From the construction of Canton Tower to planning a next-generation financial hub in Nansha New Area, we have transformed the city and expanded its influence in the Greater Bay Area. Leveraging our foresight and ingenuity, we will continue to nurture the sustainable growth of Guangzhou, helping realise its global ambition.
