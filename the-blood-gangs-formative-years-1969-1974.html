Through my personal research along with lots of research more than I had collected on my own, by Baby S Mac, we haven’t found a single newspaper article or documentary that refers to anyone as a member of the BLOOD or a BLOOD GANG.

This does NOT indicate or any intend to draw my own narrative but confirmation of what a lot of original bloods have indicated to me on and off camera. Jackamoe raises some great points, however, reading and studying thousands of newspaper articles I see the non crip gangs labeled by their original name, not bloods.
