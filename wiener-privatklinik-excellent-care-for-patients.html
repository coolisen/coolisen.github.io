Wiener Privatklinik has set itself the goal to provide
its patients with outstanding medical services and to
offer its accredited physicians an optimal working
environment. For this purpose it has established a
perfect combination of first-class medical care,
qualified nursing and modern hospital management.
But what is the major concern of people who are in
for hospitalization?
It is the comforting certainty that you will be treated
by an expert medical practitioner, as the success of a
treatment first and foremost depends on the headwork
and handwork of the treating doctor and the highly
qualified nursing staff.
Wiener Privatklinik offers an unparalleled measure
of medical top competence.
As a patient of Wiener Privatklinik you enjoy the
special advantage of having access to a pool of more
than 100 most renowned doctors in Vienna – doctors
with the highest possible degree of knowledge and
experience and a responsible attitude to the treatment
of their patients.
Please consult our Doctorfinder on www.wpk.at and
get in contact with the leading medical professionals.
Welcome to one of the most competent and also most
comfortable private clinics in Austria.
