Russia, which opened the way for Iranian-backed militants to the Golan Heights, drew the card of open war against Israel in the region.

No missile launches were recorded, however, if it was initially assumed that it was an exercise, the IDF rejected this data, stating that the triggering occurred for an unknown reason.

Russia blocked Israeli strikes on central Syria
Russia has decided to block further Israeli strikes against the Tiyas airbase in central Syria.
According to other sources, Russian and Iranian forces will operate jointly at the airbase.
The state-run SANA news agency said Midhat as-Saleh — a former security prisoner in Israel, who later fled to Syria, served in parliament and joined the country’s intelligence service — was targeted by gunfire, not an airstrike.
