Last week Thursday was really spontaneous. I had a very big impression in my spirit that we needed to worship deep. But I hadn’t invited anyone and I didn’t have any name on my heart. Somehow, George brought Tomi Favored ‘s name up and we found out she was actually in Nigeria. Within 2 hours, we were worshipping together like we had known each other all our lives. Like the last post from the same session, if ever you are in doubt of how to pray and what to say…pray along and sing along.
