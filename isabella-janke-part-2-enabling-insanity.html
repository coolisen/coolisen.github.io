Welcome to part 2 of the Isabella Janke story and documentary. In part 1 we covered and focus primarily on Isabella Janke, her actions and her beliefs. Part 2 seeks to reinforce the American Horror Story with interviews and logs of those closest to her. Her father, her friends, the Chess Club Discord server but also the actions of YouTube creator, Gibi and Wildcat aka Sean Walker.

Gibi and Sean are Chris Chan archivers and have a deep understanding and history within the Chris Chan lore. This is why Isabella Janke sought to feed them both information. Part 2 of the story explains how Sean and Gibi reacted to being spoon fed information from Bella and shows what they were both subsequently exposed for.
