Hey there all, how’s it going? I’m back with a short video inspired by some recent posts about the state of what sorts of content tends to be most readily available on the witchy youtube vlogosphere. I think it’s one of the fundamental pitfalls and limitations of this medium, and if there’s a solution for it, I haven’t found it.
