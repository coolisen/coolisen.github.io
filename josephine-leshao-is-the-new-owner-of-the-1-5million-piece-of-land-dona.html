Josephine Leshao has not known peace since she was 13.
Married at that particular age, she had to endure the worst kind of life from her husband who also infected her with HIV.
Despite also fighting cancer, Josephine’s willpower to fight and hustle for her children is absolutely admirable.
Honestly, when Lynn Ngugi partnered with Optiven Group to donate a piece of Land at the Garden of Joy worth 1.5M shillings, we did not know the journey we were about to encounter would be challenging, emotional and life changing.
We featured three extremely needy families and finally asked the public to help us identify one family who would be in dire need of the land and we are happy to announce Josephine finally has a place to call home

A huge shout out to Optiven Group led by their amazing team leader Mr. George Wachiuri for believing in my vision and giving me a chance to continue impacting the community.
