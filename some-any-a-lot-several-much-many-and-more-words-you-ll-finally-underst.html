As I told you in the first video, these group of words are not the problem. The way we learn the vocabulary is the real problem here.
That’s why we are using two colors in Triads Method ©
💜 Purple for countable vocabulary
💛 Yellow for the uncountable vocabulary
Lerning the vocabulary using these two colors will help you not making mistakes. You’ll see how it works in the next video.
