Just Found Out from Jaipur, self-made rapper and doctor, MassQline, belts out his latest hip-hop song, “Maalik Ghulam.” The track is about internalising the master-slave dichotomy and unleashing unimaginable power within oneself. The music video for “Maalik Ghulam” showcases MassQline riding solo and performing in the streets of Jaipur, singing in celebration, his song of freedom from the shackles of societal control.
