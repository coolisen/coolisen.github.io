ABOUT THIS VIDEO:
This video is about ‘Seem"
The hindi meaning of "Seem" = prateet hona, Lagna
Ex: Aisa lag raha hai ki tum pagal ho.
Aisa lag raha tha ki wo dukhi thi.
Rule[It + seems/seemed + that sub + is/are/am/was/were + ob]
Ex: It seems that you are mad.
It seemed that she was sad.
……………………………………………………………..
Advance
…………..
[Sub + seems/seemed + to be + ob}
Ex: He seems to be mad.
You seem to be upset.
He seemed to be inebriated yesterday.
