Official HD music video for “A Little Respect” by Erasure.

The third single from the number 1 album ‘The Innocents’ was released in September1988 and reached number 4 in the UK charts. The classic video was directed by Peter ‘Sleazy’ Christopherson of Throbbing Gristle, who also worked with The The, Nik Kershaw, Marc Almond, NIN, Van Halen and Mute label mate and collaborator Diamanda Galas.

The single was a hit single in 2001 for U.S band, Wheatus and also recorded by Kim Wilde and the rugby players of Hull Kingston Rovers.
