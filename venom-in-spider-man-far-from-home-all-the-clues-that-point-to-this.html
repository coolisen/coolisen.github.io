Spider-Man: Far From Home is set to contain a number of supporting and cameo appearances from other Marvel characters as the movie universe continues to grow and overlap. There are also plenty of clues that suggest that Venom will appear in the movie as well.

Fans first thought that Venom will appear in the movie when they saw Peter Parker wearing an all black spider suit. Furthermore, many remembered that Tom Holland’s Peter Parker was initially set to appear in the Venom film, so Tom Hardy’s character could appear in the MCU as well. Venom’s unexpected success only increases the chances of the character appearing in the MCU, probably through the dimensional overlap mentioned by Mysterio. Then again, Peter Parker’s time in space, the many alien devices in the Spider-Man movies, and his many off-world friends; only increases the chances of the Venom symbiote appearing in Far From Home. Then there are the meta reasons for Venom appearing in the next Spider-Man movie; like Disney and Sony needing to strengthen their relationship, Venom’s costume not making sense without Spider-Man, and Venom’s absence in Into The Spider-Verse.

Spider-Man: Far From Home is going to set the tone for the future of the Marvel Cinematic Universe following Avengers: Endgame. Even if it will only be in a small capacity, that future is absolutely set to include Venom.
