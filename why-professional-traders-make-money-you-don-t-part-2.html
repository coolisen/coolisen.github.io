Coming from a finance background and having visited trading floors of multiple investment banks, i wanna share with you how professional traders trade, how pro traders make money as well as the pro traders mindset.

By professional traders, i’m referring to hedge fund traders or institutional traders who trade large capital and not just the typical retail trader trading at home. With that said, professional trader can also be the typical retail trader who is experienced in the forex market and stock market.

A lot of new retail traders come into the markets with the wrong kind of expectations because they are fed the wrong kind of trading information. A lot of retail traders are being led by retail traders who has zero finance background and why would you learn from somebody who has never stepped onto a trading floor of an investment bank?

I have seen a lot of educators with zero finance background teaching retail traders how to trade. They have no idea how professional traders trade. I’m not saying all trading educators are like that because some are legit trading professionals but what i’m saying is that i see too many people especially on youtube, instagram and tiktok who teach trading and has zero professional trading experience to show for.

As somebody who has spent years studying how professional traders trade, there is just too much misinformation out there and i want to share with you how professional traders approach the forex or stock market so that you can change your mindset as well.
