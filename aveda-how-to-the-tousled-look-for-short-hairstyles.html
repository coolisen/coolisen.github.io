Towel dry and spritz Aveda Brilliant Damage Control onto your hair to protect from thermal damage and UV rays. At Aveda, we believe product plus technique gets results. Beenders demonstrates how to finger-dry hair for improved texture. Hold the hair taut in one direction while drying, switching to the other side to smooth frizz and natural waves.
