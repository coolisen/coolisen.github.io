In this lesson, I have explained the word "Substantial” with examples. Learn this English vocabulary word and use it in your daily conversation. It will help in improving your English Fluency.

Meaning:
Large in size, degree, value or importance
Sentences:
1. A substantial amount of people attended the seminar.
2. The report indicates a substantial increase in sales.
3. Economic reform is the most substantial matter for the government.
