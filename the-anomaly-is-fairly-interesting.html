A low-budget sci-fi that borrows elements from Memento and Total Recall sounds pretty good, on paper. Joe reviews Noah Clarke’s "The Anomaly", starring Noah Clarke, written by Noah Clarke.

Sometimes low-budget flicks surprise you, but unfortunately this one didn’t. It definitely had potential, a pretty interesting story and decent direction; but would have fared much better if it had a bigger budget and a more charismatic leading character. Overall somewhat decent if you can get past the mediocre acting and slightly messy script.

IMDb summary - A former soldier is taken captive and awakens in the back of a van where he learns that he only has a few moments to figure out how he got there.
