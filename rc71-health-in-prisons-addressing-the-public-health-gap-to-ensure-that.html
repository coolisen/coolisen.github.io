People in prison suffer disproportionally from a high prevalence of co-occurring health problems than people in the community. This event raises Member States’ awareness of the relevance of prison health as an integral part of public health and to encourage them to fulfil their obligations under the Nelson Mandela Rules and the Bangkok Rules through improved governance.
