2 Trains Passing As Seen From Above! These trains are passing on the Norfolk Southern Railroad main line between Chattanooga and Cincinnati. Look how cool they look from above! A huge mixed freight train that "emptied the yard" meets a steel roll train while I was at the perfect spot. We also see a railroad crossing on a steep grade in Walton, Kentucky on the same NS line. The trains passing are at Crittenden, Kentucky. Railroad maintenance was going on just north of here where private contractors were working with NS crews to replace private driveway crossings around MP 25 on the NS Rathole Line. Every train that came through had to get permission to pass the area over the radio so it was easy to know when a train was approaching. There are also several defect detectors in the area and all communications were on `160.950. I heard both trains on the radio so I figured I could get a cool video from the bridge just south of the construction zone.
