In this video, learn how to use "few" and "a few".

Both are used to talk about a small quantity of something. However, they have slightly different meanings.

You can think of "few" as not many. It emphasizes the lack of something.

You can think of "a few" as "some". It emphasizes the existence of something (as a small quantity).

Learn more about "few" and "a few" in this lesson.
