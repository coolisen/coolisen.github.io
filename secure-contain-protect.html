For those asking about the SCP, it’s not based on a specific subject from the archives. It may resemble the description of some SCPs, but know he was created specifically for this video. Since the original MTF models are pretty low quality, I went ahead and used the FBI characters from BO2. Hope that clears up some of the confusion.


For the best experience, watch in 4k with headphones.

"S.C.P." is an experimental CGI piece based on Call of Duty: Black Ops II and SCP: Containment Breach. The project was put in motion about 15 months ago, spending the majority of the time on polishing the animation and composition work. I learned a lot of techniques as I progressed through the the video, and intend to keep learning to better my upcoming projects. Lastly, I would really like to thank all of you guys that support what I do. Knowing that I have your guys’ support and interest in my videos really motivates me to keep going. Thank you all.
