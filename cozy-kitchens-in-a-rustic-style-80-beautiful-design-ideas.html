Cozy kitchens in a rustic style! 80 beautiful design ideas!

Rustic kitchen design is a solution that can harmoniously complement the interior of a country house or create an appropriate atmosphere in a city apartment.
Rustic style is always a natural palette. It can be either neutral (white, beige, brown, gray and their shades), or brighter (rich green, cheerful yellow, accent red), or it can be subtle and delicate, based on pastel colors (mint, peach, dusty -pink).
The stylistics base is natural materials: wood, metal, ceramics, wicker accessories. Obvious artificiality (plastic, polyurethane, unnatural linoleum) will definitely not come to court.
It is better to lay out the floor in the country-style kitchen with ceramic tiles, porcelain stoneware, trim with wood or cork.
Walls - leave wooden (if you are decorating a wooden house), paint or decorate with wooden clapboard, aged boards. You can also turn to wallpaper with plant or floral patterns if you gravitate towards Provence.
Wooden beams will be appropriate on the ceiling; full wood trim also looks impressive; but you can choose just a flat, light ceiling.
