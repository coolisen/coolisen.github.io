🌹Scared to raise your hand in class? Hate getting called out by your teacher? Wondering what to do if you’re shy in class? I’VE BEEN THERE! BUT, as I got older I realized how important it is to be able to talk in a room of people and how participating in class is a really great skill to develop. SO whether you’re in online class or in-person class, I’ll be sharing 5 TIPS I used that you can use too, in order to overcome your shyness and truly break out of your shell… and finally learn how to feel comfortable participating in class🌹
