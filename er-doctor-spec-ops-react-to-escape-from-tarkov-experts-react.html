Cameron and Jordan (what a combo!) are with Gamology today reviewing the much asked for: Escape From Tarkov. As a result of political scandals and corporate collapses, Tarkov has become a mess and warring factions have turned the city into a shell of its former self with sections of the city being controlled by aggressive locals called "scavs".﻿
