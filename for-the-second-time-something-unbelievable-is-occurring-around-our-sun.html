Without a doubt, the sun is the most influential celestial body in our galactic neighborhood. We owe our very development on our home plant solely to the constant supply of energy from our host star. The subject of this video is these objects spotted in the sun’s vicinity and the unbelievable conclusions supporters of alien theories draw from them.
