As you know, Jimmy is much more than just a beloved TV talk show host, he is also a respected TV Judge. For the past 35 years, he has been ruling on cases with the help of his trusty bailiff Guillermo and after a long break from the law, they are back in the courtroom. Since Tiffany had shown a photo of Meagan Good as an example of what she wanted, we invited Meagan to the courtroom to help with the ruling.
