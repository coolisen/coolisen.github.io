Teach and learn school supplies with our school supplies song for kids. This is a great song for children to learn the English vocabulary for school objects and classroom objects.
Vocabulary in this song: pencil, ruler, notebook, marker, stapler and paper. Children will also learn to say the language structure "I have a ~" and the question form, "What do you have?". Teach and learn English classroom words with Fun Kids English video. Great for young children, ESL/EFL students, or babies. It’s EASY to sing along and young learners will love it.
