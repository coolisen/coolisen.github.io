Sky News host Paul Murray says ageism is the most obvious "ism" that exists in Australia.

"The attitude towards people of a certain age, and that age is increasingly getting younger, is horrific," Mr Murray said.

It comes as the Human Rights Commission has revealed 60 per cent of Australians agree they may have stereotyped people or made assumptions about people because of their age.
