For builders, plan reviewers, inspectors, and municipal and state administrators, online or electric permitting systems can streamline and expedite the construction process while helping municipalities ensure code compliance and enforcement. This webinar discussed electronic permitting systems in use by building departments across the land and how they might apply to your jurisdiction.

This was the second event in the 2020 Building Energy Code Webinar Series. More information is available at https://www.energycodes.gov/2020-building-energy-code-webinar-series.
