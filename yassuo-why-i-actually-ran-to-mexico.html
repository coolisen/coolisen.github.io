But now that players, and Moe, have had more time to figure Akshan out, Moe can give a more honest and accurate opinion on the champion.

With this being the end of the season, Moe decided to give his thoughts on the overall 2021 LCS season and breakdown the playoffs to see how 100 Thieves took the Summoner’s Cup!
Finally, with Moe running away to Mexico and trying to grind on the LAN server from Unranked to Challenger.
