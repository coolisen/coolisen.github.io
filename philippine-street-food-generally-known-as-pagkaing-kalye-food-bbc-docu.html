Philippine Street Food, generally known as "Pagkaing Kalye" in Filipino, is a wide selection of affordable cuisine available from vendors or peddlers on the streets. It is basically influenced by other countries like Chinese and Japanese, the food was taken to the next level and given a Filipino perspective.
