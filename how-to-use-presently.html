Select the shapes on the mockup frame and fill them with the image you have exported
You can also make adjustments to colors to suit your design


Method 2

From your Figma desktop app, select the UI frame you have designed, right-click and copy as PNG
Open presently
Select the shape of your chosen frame and paste (ctrl + v)
You can also make adjustments to colors to suit your design
