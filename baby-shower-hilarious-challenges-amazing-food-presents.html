I know I said this a lot, but in my mind, this baby shower was going to be the best party next to my wedding day. I’m THRILLED to say that it WAS! This party was everything I dreamed of and more. My decorations were absolutely perfect and unlike anything I have ever had at a party in my life–honestly prettier than my wedding!! I’m so thankful for family and friends who came and for family and friends who helped with my very special day. I also want to thank people who travelled quite far in order to set up decorations and food at my house!! I know it took one person over 2 hours, but we REALLY appreciate your amazing decorating talents. So many people really came together on this and I want to thank you all from the bottom of my heart!
