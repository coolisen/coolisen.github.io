Maintain your salon style with Color Conserve Shampoo and Conditioner, keeping hair vibrant with plant-infused ingredients that won’t strip pigment like harsher cleansers do. To ensure long-lasting style, finger-comb Aveda Phomollient from roots to ends and dry on high-heat and high speed until about 60% dry.

At Aveda, we believe product plus technique gets results. Beenders demonstrates how to finger-dry hair for improved texture. Hold the hair taut in one direction while drying, switching to the other side to smooth frizz and natural waves.
