Learning Targets:
I can explain the difference between a.m. and p.m.
I can translate time on an analog clock to a digital clock.
I can draw hands on an analog clock using time on a digital clock.
I can write the time and draw in the hands on an analog clock when someone tells me what time it is to the nearest 5 minutes.
I can describe special terms such as: half past, quarter after/past, quarter to, minutes after/past, minutes to.
