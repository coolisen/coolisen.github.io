Be crafty and make cool items for your home without spending a ton of money! You can easily decorate your home only using a hot glue gun! You don’t believe us? Check the video!
The hot glue gun is one of the best purchases you have ever made. Why? Because you can fix anything using a hot glue gun and also make cool crafts with minimum effort. Let’s check the coolest glue gun crafts: create a cool decoration for your home - hot glue waterfall; decorate an old t-shirt; make cute needle tops; fix your glasses in 5 minutes; you can create jewelry that will suit your personal taste and style.

Rubber bands are super multifunctional items you can use everywhere. Place a rubber band on top of a paint can to remove the excess paint from the brush. Do not throw away your old keyboard! There are so many ways to reuse it. Our useful lifehacks will help you to reuse your old keyboard in an unexpected way – make fun jewelry or minimalistic clocks! Cracked heels are a common foot problem for every girl who loves to wear high heels. The cheapest treatment is lemon! Firstly, cut the lemon in half (squeeze the juice). Then, apply half a lemon directly on the heel. After doing this, take a pair of socks to keep on the feet the compress for 30 minutes.
