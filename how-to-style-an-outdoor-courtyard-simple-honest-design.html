Janie Allen lived in her townhouse for five years and had never used the courtyard because she didn’t know how it COULD be enjoyed. It was a weird space with tall walls, mismatched flooring, and chipping paint. So she called in the big guns (that would be me) to help her figure out how to make it a space she would enjoy every day from the inside, outside, and even by walking by the gate on her way to the front door. Check out how we made it happen on this week’s episode of Simple.Honest.Design
