I know I said this a lot, but in my mind, this baby shower was going to be the best party next to my wedding day. I’m THRILLED to say that it WAS! This party was everything I dreamed of and more. My decorations were absolutely perfect and unlike anything I have ever had at a party in my life–honestly prettier than my wedding!! I’m so thankful for family and friends who came and for family and friends who helped with my very special day. My sister in law is an AMAZING bow tier (I probably said that in a weird way, but she is!) and she tied all the bows you’ll see around the shower. My mom and sisters made the cupcakes and cookies, my amazing editor Courtney filmed this for me, everyone came together and it was just perfect!! I also want to thank everyone who came for being covid tested prior to the shower. I was truly worried people wouldn’t come because of that, but everyone still came! Thank you all for the wonderful gifts for our baby girl—everything is gorgeous and SO helpful since we are starting from scratch with this beautiful baby girl of ours!!!
I also want to thank people who travelled quite far in order to set up decorations and food at my house!! I know it took one person over 2 hours, but we REALLY appreciate your amazing decorating talents. We also appreciate overnight shipping to get some of the decorations to our house that we put up ourselves. So many people really came together on this and I want to thank you all from the bottom of my heart!
