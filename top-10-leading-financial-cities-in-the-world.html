The Leading Financial Cities in the world ranking according to the Global Financial Centre Index.
Which one is the global leader? London, New York or maybe Hong Kong? Find out by watching the video.
Financial centres are locations with a concentration of participants in banking, asset management, insurance or financial markets with venues and supporting services for these activities to take place.
The Global Financial Centres Index (GFCI) is a ranking of the competitiveness of financial centres based on over 29,000 financial centre assessments from an online questionnaire together with over 100 indices from organisations such as the World Bank, the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD), and the Economist Intelligence Unit
