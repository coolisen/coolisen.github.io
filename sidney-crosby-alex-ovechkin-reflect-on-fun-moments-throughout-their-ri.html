For just the second time in their careers — and first since their rookie seasons — Sidney Crosby and Alexander Ovechkin sit down together for an interview. Colby Armstrong caught up to the duo at the NHL’s Player Media Tour in Chicago last month. They talk about chasing Wayne Gretzky’s scoring record, their iconic dueling hat-trick playoff game, and their less-than iconic commercial acting forays.
