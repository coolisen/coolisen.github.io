Ingredients:
for the base:
glazed biscuits with cherry filling - 200 g (7 oz)

for the cheese composition:
cow cheese - 1 kg (2.2 lb)
cream - 600 ml (20.3 fl oz)
vanilla sugar - 15 g (½ oz)
eggs - 6 pieces
vanilla-flavored pudding powder - 80 g (2.82 oz)
for the glaze:
cream - 100 g (3.5 oz)
chocolate - 100 g (3.5 oz)
glazed biscuits with cherry filling - 100 g (3.5 oz)

Tray diameter 24 cm (9.45 in)
To cold 1 h
In the oven 180°C (356 °F) for 45 min
