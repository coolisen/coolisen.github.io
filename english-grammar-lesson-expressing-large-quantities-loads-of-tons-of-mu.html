Learning English Grammar is always tricky and confuses students, particularly when talking about large quantities. In today’s English Lesson with Michelle, you will learn how to use the terms - Loads of, A lots of, Tons of, Hundreds of and Much and Many while talking about plural nouns in English. Pay close attention to this lesson so that you don’t make some common English speaking mistakes while talking about quantities.
