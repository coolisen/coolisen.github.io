Surrender was our next choice since it was both a Billy Talent song (therefore Mark already knew it by heart, no question) and a sufficiently old one (so that Milton would be somewhat familiar with the tune through years of unintentional exposure).
No explosions or slow-mo in this video, but we do have Mark singing for the first time on camera and simultaneously playing guitar no less!
