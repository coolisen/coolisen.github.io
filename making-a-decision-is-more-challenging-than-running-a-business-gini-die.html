In this video, we have shared the inspiring journey of the Founder of Spin Sucks, Gini Dietrich.

Gini is a communicator by trade who has grown in a big PR firm world. She has worked for Fleishman Hillard for many years and moved to an ad agency to build their PR department and later, started her own agency.
