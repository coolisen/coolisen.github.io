1. Clean the gunk under your nails - run tool up and under each nail.

2. Clip - gnawed and bitten nails are nasty. If you are biting your nails, you must stop! Long nails are equally nasty! Clip by following the natural contour of the nail bed and leaving a bit of nail.

3. Smooth - using the file, smooth off the nail so that rouge hangnails are removed.
