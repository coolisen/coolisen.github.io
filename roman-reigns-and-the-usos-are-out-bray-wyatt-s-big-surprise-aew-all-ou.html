Roman Reigns and The Usos are on top of WWE Smackdown as The Bloodline. Smackdown mostly revolves around Roman Reigns and The Usos. The Usos and Roman Reigns are a part of many Smackdown storylines in WWE, but Smackdown may be without their biggest stars of Roman Reigns and The Usos for their first big UK tour. WWE will be touring the UK with the Smackdown roster but The Bloodline, Roman Reigns and The Usos reportedly won’t be a part of that tour for Smackdown. Roman Reigns and The Usos will instead be joining the WWE Raw roster that week on September 20th.
