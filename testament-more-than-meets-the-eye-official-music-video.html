For this brand new record, "The Formation Of Damnation", the band has brought their original lineup back together – vocalist Chuck Billy, guitarists Eric Peterson & Alex Skolnick, and bassist Greg Christian – and have added friend and former member, Paul Bostaph.

"It’s come full circle. We were together, moving strong, then we broke up and had several different lineups," says Billy. "But now we got a new band, a new label, a new album, everything’s positive."

The Formation of Damnation has since sold over 84,000 copies in the U.S and as of November 2011 it sold over 300,000 copies worldwide
