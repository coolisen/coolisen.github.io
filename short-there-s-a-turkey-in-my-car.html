There’s a Turkey in My Car
And I can’t get him out.
I can’t drive with him too far
He won’t listen when I shout.
Hey turkey won’t you fly away?
Don’t tell me you can’t fly.
I forgot that turkeys can’t talk either.
I must bid you goodbye.
When I stop the car, get out
And wobble fast, you hear?
Please don’t come back till next Thanksgiving,
That’s about a year.
You need to go back to your nest
Way up in that tree.
Oh, don’t say that can’t be your nest.
“That’s a bird’s nest. Can’t you see?”
I forgot that turkey’s seldom fly,
Can’t talk and stay well-grounded.
But let’s be clear. If you’re still here
Next year, I’ll be astounded!
