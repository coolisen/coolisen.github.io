But we have to face time Jenny and see the new baby first. Oh, she is sooo cute!!

We get to the lake and the kids run as fast as they can to the beach. They love our new pirate ship and even though we played on it for hours yesterday, they can’t wait to play pretend some more today. We do have to clean up and mop a little, because some sand has got inside the pirate ship. We head home to see Jenny, who has some amazing baby updates and we end the vlog playing with marbles in the ultimate toy room!!
