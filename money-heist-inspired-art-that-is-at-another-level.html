Money Heist is a Spanish heist crime drama television series created by Álex Pina. The series traces two long-prepared heists led by the Professor, told from the perspective of one of the robbers, Tokyo. Once on Netflix, the series got a massive success, and now it has fans all around the globe. In this episode, we bring you some of the best art inspired by the series. Enjoy!
