We get to the lake and the kids run as fast as they can to the beach. They love our new pirate ship and even though we played on it for hours yesterday, they can’t wait to play pretend some more today. We do have to clean up and mop a little, because some sand has got inside the pirate ship. Adley and Niko do get caught by pirates and trapped in jail, but luckily I was able to find the keys to free them.
