It has been about a year since we launched the White Line boards. No hassle with all kinds of options and choices, just beautiful boards in a proven strong construction and well thought out shapes. You can’t really go wrong with these kiteboards.

Our team of riders also love having a white line board next to their custom boards. Not only because of the classic white color, but especially because the boards have a nice feel. This season Roderick and his Malus Domestica White Line were inseparable. Beautifully captured by Lennart Films.

Want to know more about the White Line kiteboards? Check out our White Line construction video or go to our website: https://appletreesurfboards.com/shop/category/kiteboards/
