Why is the interviewer asking you this question?
“We all have to make difficult decisions at work. If you can make difficult decisions that are in the best interests of the organization, you are far more likely to get hired!”

3 TIPS FOR ANSWERING THIS DIFFICULT INTERVIEW QUESTION:
#1. Show you have the CONFIDENCE to make difficult decisions.
#2. In your answer, demonstrate you always put the organization first.
#3. When making difficult decisions, show that you always consider your options carefully.
