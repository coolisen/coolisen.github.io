The Audi Q4 e-tron is the German brand’s most affordable electric car so far, and shares its MEB platform with the VW ID.4 and Skoda Enyaq. This pure-electric mid-size family SUV is available with 52kWh or 77kWh batteries to give claimed ranges of between 208 and 316 miles respectively. In this CarGurus UK video, Vicky Parrot tests what we rate as the pick of the range: the Q4 e-tron 40 with rear-wheel drive, the larger battery, 201bhp, and 0-62mph time of 8.1 seconds.
