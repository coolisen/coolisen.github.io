I am introducing two simple and best homemade fertilizers in this video. One is used for curry tree/ curry leaves & another one for flowering.

Fertilizer for curry leaves.

1 tbsp yogurt diluted in 2 cups of water can be poured around the base of curry tree. Use it in three weeks interval for best results. Make sure that the soil is dry before application.


Fertilizer for flowering plants.

Used tea leaves/ bags (dried)
Banana peel/ Banana skin (dried)
Egg shells (dried)

Mix ground ingredients (1tsp each) to make the fertilizer.

If you have difficulty to dry the waste (ingredients) you can grind it together and dilute it with water to form the fertilizer.
