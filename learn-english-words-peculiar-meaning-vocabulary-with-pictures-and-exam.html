What does Peculiar mean? Learn the meaning of Peculiar as we define this advanced vocabulary word with a simple definition, pictures, example sentences, English pronunciation and audio.

Peculiar definition (adjective) odd; unusual

Peculiar pronunciation: pi-kyool-yer

Examples of Peculiar in a sentence:

1. My uncle is a peculiar man who wears the same outfit several days a week.

2. Whenever I smell something peculiar coming from the kitchen, I know it is best to go to a friend’s house for dinner.

3. The medicine made me feel peculiar as though I was walking on needles.

4. Once I told my doctor the medicine made me feel peculiar, he altered my prescription.

5. My peculiar sister has twenty-three cats.

Peculiar synonyms and other related words for Peculiar

odd, strange, unusual, weird, bizarre, curious, queer, singular, extraordinary, eccentric, abnormal, uncommon, offbeat, freakish, unconventional, exceptional, outlandish, unique, different, funny, special, individual, particular, idiosyncratic, kinky, quaint, unnatural, fantastic, freaky, aberrant
