"Funky Green Dogs" are more or less quiet well known under another pseudonym which is "Murk". At the end it´s Ralph Falcón and Oscar Gaetan who are responsible for a lot of successful releases.

In 1996 they released their debut on "Twisted Records US". A really huge anthem which was also got remixes- for example from the living legend "Danny Tenaglia". The sigle was also part of the album called "Get Fired Up".

The label Twisted, which had a huge impact in the 90ies regarding house music was founded by "Rob Di Stefano & Mark Davenport" in 1996 and was really successful from the beginning. We met Rob Di Stefano a few times. Brilliant guy with lots of passion…
