The first flight of a slow stick plane I scratch built using the wing from Flitetest’s FT Old Fogey.

Apparently the mental demands of flying a new plane, filming at the same time and trying not to hit the people walking their dogs across the green wiped out the bit of my brain that remembers not to try to land downwind. It’ll buff out :)

Stats: Blue wonder 1700kv motor, 9 x 4.7 prop, Plush 18A ESC, Turnigy 2S 1300mAh LiPo, salvaged radio/servos, Depron wing, wood fuse. Total weight is about 450g
