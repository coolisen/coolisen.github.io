What makes a stock a hype stock? In this video, we’ll talk about the commonalities among hype stocks, such as overvalued stocks and pump and dump scenarios with lots of YouTube channels advocating for a stock (especially young people). We’ll talk about how to avoid the hype–including high-flying stocks like Tesla (TSLA), NIO, and Netflix (NFLX)–and stick to a good process for value investing and trading.
