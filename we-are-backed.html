This film chronicles the past 12 months of life on the good ship Backed. It celebrates the founders, innovators, tech optimists, investors and pirates we proudly sail alongside in these challenging times. BACKED is the human-centric venture capital fund. We invest in exceptional founders, at Seed, in Europe. Put People First		 Be An Apprentice Push the Limits Bring Good Energy. Since 2015.
