Why our education is this serious, when scientists already proved that the best way to knowledge is to have fun? A wonderful and inspiring talk about our life in education and our education in life.

Why so serious?

Jean Philippe Rosier is an educator and partner of Perestroika, a school of creative activities that just arrived in Europe. During his career, he attended to LEGO Serious Play, Kaospilot School and Holacracy. In TEDxIEMadrid he will share his vision on how experience drives education.

This talk was given at a TEDx event using the TED conference format but independently organized by a local community. Learn more at https://www.ted.com/tedx
