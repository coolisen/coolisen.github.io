Although Tzeentch resides within the Realm of Chaos, his attention is focused predominantly on the mortal world. The contradictions, hypocrisy, and deceit scarring the souls of mortals acts as a lure to the Changer of Ways. In some cases, he uses his mortal pawns in the Great Game against the other Ruinous Powers. However, he is mostly interested in satisfying his primal desire to control and twist the fates’ of every being as they become embroiled in his incomprehensible and infinite schemes.
