Hey there all, how’s it going? I’m back with a short video inspired by some recent posts about the state of what sorts of content tends to be most readily available on the witchy youtube vlogosphere. And, more specifically, about the barrier presented by a desire for privacy when it comes to producing content, and how this desire can lead to the perception of shallowness. I think it’s one of the fundamental pitfalls and limitations of this medium, and if there’s a solution for it, I haven’t found it.
