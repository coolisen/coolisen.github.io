This lesson’s about an English word family - economy, economize, economic, economical, economist, economics.

They’re words you’ll often see in the news and they’re useful if you’re going to use English at work or take an exam like IELTS or TOEIC.

We work through the words in the economy word family looking at their different meanings, uses and pronunciations. (For example, the pronunciation of economy and economic is tricky because the word stress changes.)
