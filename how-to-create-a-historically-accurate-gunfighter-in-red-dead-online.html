Today we’re looking at gunfighters in the American West. For this week I used a variety of photo archives from the late-1800s, and the Library of Congress’ Newspaper Archives. As for secondary sources, I used Jeremy Agnew’s ‘The Old West in Fact and Film: History vs Hollywood,’ James Reasoner’s ‘Draw: The Greatest Gunfights of the American West,’ Paula Mitchel Marks’ ‘And Die in the West: The Story of the OK Corral Gunfight,’ and Jack Weston’s ‘The Real American Cowboy’, among others. Additionally, I utilized direct quotations from Stuart Lake’s interviews with Wyatt Earp in 1910.
