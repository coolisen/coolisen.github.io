After watching this video you will be able to understand how to use ‘Turn out to be" in English properly.
……………………………………………………………………………………………………

Turn out to be = Something or someone becomes something eventually.

Rule [Sub + h.v. + turn out to be + other words]

Ex:
My friend turned out to be my enemy.
My fear turned out to be groundless.
The news turned out to be false.
