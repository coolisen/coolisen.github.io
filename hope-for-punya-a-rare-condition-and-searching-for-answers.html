Punya is diagnosed with Phelan-Mcdermid syndrome and does not currently speak. Ananya, her sister, is dedicated to learning how Punya communicates so the two can better connect. Ananya even sometimes wears Punya’s old dresses in order to feel close to her beloved sister

Two years ago Punya started having seizures that have severely impacted her life. Her family is now searching for answers.
