How A True INFJ Introduce Themselves To People. INFJs prefer spending most of their time alone, but when they’re obliged to meet people, they manifest behavior patterns that are different from how others usually introduce themselves. So, how do true INFJs introduce themselves to people?

Just like a deep lake surrounded by shallow waters, INFJs feel confined and trapped in social settings, especially when they’re obliged to interact with a few strangers. But as they condition themselves to internalize their “people-person” character, they could get some strangers to drink a few beers with them after a few hours of talking. As an INFJ, do you also plan ahead how you introduce yourself to strangers? Do these situations still make you nervous?
