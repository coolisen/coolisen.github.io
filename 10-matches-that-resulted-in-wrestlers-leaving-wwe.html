WWE has had several wrestlers leave the company for one reason or the other over the years. The company has lost wrestlers multiple times over the years, and the mass departure from WWE has never been seen more than recently with the hundreds of wrestlers released in recent times.
Only in 2021, there have been 71 wrestlers released from the company.
However, even before this over the years, certain matches and angles booked by WWE frustrated stars to the point where they were ready to leave the company. On the other side, sometimes their performances in certain matches led to wrestlers being released from the company.
With that being the case, this is Wrestling Hub, and in this video, we will talk about matches that directly or indirectly led to wrestlers leaving WWE.
