Maintain your salon style with Color Conserve Shampoo and Conditioner, keeping hair vibrant with plant-infused ingredients that won’t strip pigment like harsher cleansers do. Towel dry and spritz Aveda Brilliant Damage Control onto your hair to protect from thermal damage and UV rays. To ensure long-lasting style, finger-comb Aveda Phomollient from roots to ends and dry on high-heat and high speed until about 60% dry.

At Aveda, we believe product plus technique gets results. Beenders demonstrates how to finger-dry hair for improved texture. Hold the hair taut in one direction while drying, switching to the other side to smooth frizz and natural waves. When hair is completely dry, finish with Aveda Control Paste to create a playful, pliable and piecey look.
