Tube & Berger release their first EP on Suara after two magnifique remixes for Pleausekraft & Belocca and AFFKT in 2012. "Pleasure Dip" is also their first record after "Lovebreak", the tune who reached #1 Overall Beatport Top100 and after 4 months is still on the Deep House Top100.
