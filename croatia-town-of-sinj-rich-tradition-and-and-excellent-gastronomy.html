The hinterland of Split Riviera is the continental part of Dalmatia. It consists of towns located mostly on rivers with
extremely rich ancient and old Croatian heritage. Sinj is a town in Croatia, which belongs to the Split-Dalmatia County. It is the center of the Cetina region. The total area is 194.27 km2. Sinj and its surroundings have 24,832 inhabitants, while the town itself has 11,448 inhabitants.
