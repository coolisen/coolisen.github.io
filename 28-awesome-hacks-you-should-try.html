WEIRD CRAFTS FROM ORDINARY THINGS

Be crafty and make cool items for your home without spending a ton of money! You can easily decorate your home only using a hot glue gun! You don’t believe us? Check the video!
The hot glue gun is one of the best purchases you have ever made. Why? Because you can fix anything using a hot glue gun and also make cool crafts with minimum effort. Let’s check the coolest glue gun crafts: create a cool decoration for your home - hot glue waterfall; decorate an old t-shirt; make cute needle tops; fix your glasses in 5 minutes; you can create jewelry that will suit your personal taste and style.
Surprise your boyfriend and show him DIY racing games gadget made by yourself! Make a stand for your iPad from pensile and a paper clip. Rubber bands are super multifunctional items you can use everywhere. Place a rubber band on top of a paint can to remove the excess paint from the brush. Use rubber bands and a plastic container to make a perfect organizer for mascara and lipsticks of different colors. If eyeglasses tend to slip off your nose, take a couple of rubber bands and watch our tutorial!
Do not throw away your old keyboard! There are so many ways to reuse it. Our useful lifehacks will help you to reuse your old keyboard in an unexpected way – make fun jewelry or minimalistic clocks! You can rip off keyboard buttons and make a cute necklace with it or a romantic message in a picture frame to your boyfriend.
Cracked heels are a common foot problem for every girl who loves to wear high heels. The cheapest treatment is lemon! Firstly, cut the lemon in half (squeeze the juice). Then, apply half a lemon directly on the heel. After doing this, take a pair of socks to keep on the feet the compress for 30 minutes.
