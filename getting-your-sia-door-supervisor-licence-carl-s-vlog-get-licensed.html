Hopefully this gives a little insight to how the SIA Door Supervisor Course Unit’s can break down, how in-depth the training is, physical intervention, searching techniques and an overview of what you will learn on an SIA Door Supervisor Course with Get Licensed.

I chose the SIA Door Supervisor as it’s the most popular, flexible and comes with First Aid module.
