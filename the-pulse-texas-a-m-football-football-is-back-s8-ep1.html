We kickoff season 8 of "The Pulse" as the Aggies welcome back a full capacity crowd into Kyle Field when they took on the Kent State Golden Flashes.

The 12th Man Productions staff also delivers personal features that include visits to the players’ hangouts and hometowns for a truly insider experience.
