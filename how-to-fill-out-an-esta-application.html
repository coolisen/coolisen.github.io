ESTA stands for Electronic System for Travel Authorization and is an entry permit for the USA as part of the Visa Waiver Program.

The ESTA application can only be submitted online and allows you to stay in the USA for up to 90 days - without a visa!

Detailed information on the ESTA application can be found at https://www.esta-application.com/esta-application/

Test with our ESTA Check with just a few clicks whether you qualify for visa-free entry to the USA: https://www.esta-application.com/esta-visa-check/

If you want to travel to the USA visa-free by plane or ship or if you want to change planes in the USA, you have to apply for ESTA in advance.

We at ESTA-ONLINE Service recommend you to take care of the application in time - ideally about 6 - 8 weeks before your departure to the United States.

You can check your ESTA status at any time in your customer account.

To fill out the ESTA application, you should be prepared with the necessary information about yourself, your employer, and details about your trip. The processing time takes only about 20 minutes.

You will need to provide information about yourself, passport information, the destination, a contact person in the US, your current employer, and an emergency contact.

In addition, questions about your health and the purpose of your trip are standard with the ESTA application.

Your address in the USA and your email address can be changed later.
We carefully check all information in your ESTA application for accuracy before forwarding it to the US authorities. This avoids an unnecessary rejection.
