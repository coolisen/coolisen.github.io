This video is about an advance English structure "PRIOR TO" which is used for the following situation:

Ex: शादी से पहले उसका चक्कर था।

थप्पड़ मारने से पहले वह उससे बात कर रही थी।

Structure[Sentence + prior to + noun/gerund]

Ex: She had an affair prior to marriage.

She was talking to him prior to slapping.
