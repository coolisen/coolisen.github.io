This film chronicles the past 12 months of life on the good ship Backed. It celebrates the founders, innovators, tech optimists, investors and pirates we proudly sail alongside in these challenging times. The reason we do this job is simple: for the fulfilment that comes from working with such visionary, gritty, good humans. Onwards and upwards.
