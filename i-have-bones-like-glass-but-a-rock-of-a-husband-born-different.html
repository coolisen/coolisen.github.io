A WOMAN with bones so fragile they’ve broken over 50 times wants to show the world bravery comes in all shapes and sizes. Stephania Gonzales, 30, was born with Osteogenesis Imperfecta, a genetic disorder which affects the strength of bones. Living in Jacksonville, Florida, with her husband, Steph doesn’t let her disability limit her from pursuing her dreams.
