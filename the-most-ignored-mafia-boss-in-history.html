He created a commission of organized crime bosses, the original bosses were 4 bosses from NYC, Joseph Bonanno, Tommy Gagliano, Vincent Mangano and Joseph Profaci. The 5th boss was the infamous Al Capone in Chicago. The 6th boss was Stefano Magaddino, known as the Buffalo mafia boss, who actually lived in and operated out of Niagara Falls, New York. Niagara Falls, NY used to be primarily Sicilian. Most people have heard of Lucky Luciano, Al Capone and maybe Joe Bonanno. Some people have heard of Joe Profaci, Vincent Mangano and Tommy Gagliano..
