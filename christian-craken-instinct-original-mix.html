Christian Stoynev (better known as Christian Craken) is a DJ/Producer and Label Owner born in Bulgaria. Christian begins his electronic music journey in 2010 after releasing numerous DJ podcasts on the popular Bulgarian platform, Vibes Radio. Not long after his first steps as a performer, Christian Craken starts making his own beats too. Nowadays, Craken releases quality underground music on significant digital labels such as Dark Face Recordings, Bully Beatz,Rubik’s Recordings, Plazma Records, and his own imprint PHOBIA Music Recordings. After successful productions released in very short term, Christian makes his solid breakthrough and performs alongside artists like: Victor Ruiz, Tomcraft, Cosmic Boys, Maksim Dark, Dani Sbert, Eric Sneo, Gaga,Mateo!, Stiv Hey and others. His appearance at some of the major Bulgarian clubs and events has become prominent in the last few years and yet is more to come.
