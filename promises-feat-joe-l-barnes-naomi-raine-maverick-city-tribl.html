Official Music Video for "Promises" featuring Joe L Barnes and Naomi Raine by Maverick City Music.

"Promises" was written by Joe L Barnes, Carrington Gaines, Keilla Alvarado, Lemuel Marin, Aaron Moses, and Dante Bowe during a Maverick City Music camp last year.
