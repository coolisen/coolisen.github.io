At InViLab, we develop machine vision techniques that tackle challenges in industry and society. Together with our partners, we create new imaging techniques for smart health, pollution monitoring, heritage conservation and sustainable materials. In industry, our ambition is to guide companies towards realizing better quality control, automation and safety through state-of-the-art computer vision and machine learning techniques.
