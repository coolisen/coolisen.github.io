I started spearfishing this year and while I am still very new, I love it. So I booked a charter to try spear fishing off the Outer Banks of North Carolina for the first time. I made some mistakes (a few small mistakes and a few big mistakes) and a fish tried to killed me. But despite that, I caught cobia, amber jack, almaco jack and spade fish. I also made three awesome fish recipes.
