Town of Salem is a social deduction game similar to Among us. Among Us allows you to play the game with up to 4-10 players, Town of Salem allows up to 15 players in a single game.
Just like Among Us, you can be good or evil with factions such as Town, Mafia and Neutrals. Among us has 2 roles (without mods), town of salem has 33 different characters in the base game. Town of Salem is a great alternative to Among Us if you’re looking for another game in the social deduction genre. If you enjoy Among Us, definitely give Town of Salem a try!
