As we are kept busy with the outrage over the #IndiaVsPak match loss & the ever winding drama of the #AryanKhanDrugsCase…here’s something about which we should really be screaming our heads off #PetrolPriceHike. What’s the REAL REASON for Petrol being on fire and with crude rising & tax loot not stopping - could be we looking at Petrol at Rs.200?

High Petrol and Diesel prices was one of the many reasons behind the wrath that Manmohan Singh Government had to face from the citizens. Today, Petrol and Diesel prices have skyrocketed and crossed Rs. 100 mark…moving steadily towards achieving the double century but there is hardly any outrage. The high prices have been blamed on Nehru, Oil bonds and even on the Corona vaccine.

The Deshbhakt in today’s episode looks at the real reason behind the high prices i.e. high levy of local taxes…How citizens are being duped by charging Taxes as high that today for Rs. 100 petrol one just gets petrol worth Rs. 36 …the rest 64 goes in taxes…Too Much Ache Din!!!
