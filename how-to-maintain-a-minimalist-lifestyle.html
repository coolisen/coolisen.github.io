Today, I want to talk about maintaining a minimalist lifestyle over a long time. While some people just enjoy decluttering and are not interested in a simpler life, a lot of us want to live an intentional life, choosing only the things that add value and decluttering the rest for good. Since decluttering once, obviously doesn’t make us a minimalist right away, this video has some tips and thoughts to continue and improve our practice at minimalism.
