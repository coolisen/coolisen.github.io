For many independent artists and crafters, Etsy has been a life-changer. By simplifying the process of running an online store, Etsy has provided a means for its sellers to turn their hobbies into careers. However, Etsy has its downsides as well. Etsy’s fee structure, in particular, gets a lot of criticism from sellers. That’s why we’re going to describe the eight different types of fees all Etsy merchants need to be aware of.
