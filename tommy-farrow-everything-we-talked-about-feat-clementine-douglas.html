Returning to Stress for his third release, Tommy Farrow delivers with, ‘Everything We Talked About’, a dreamy breakbeat release featuring vocalist, Clementine Douglas.

‘Everything We Talked About’ begins with Burial esque drum patterns, distant vocals, and melancholic synths, before breaking down with a stark piano line, progressing into a crescendo of steel drums and Celmentine Douglas’ powerful vocals. The track is beautifully deep and very refreshing.

Sheffield-based, Tommy Farrow is no stranger to Stress and his consistently strong releases are set to make even more waves in the dance scene.
