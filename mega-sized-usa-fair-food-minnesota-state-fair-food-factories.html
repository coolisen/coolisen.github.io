3. CORN ROAST

ROASTED SWEET CORN: Soak corn for 10-15 minutes. Place on the grill for 10 minutes then flip it and let it roast another 5 minutes at 700 degrees F. Add water to steam it then husk. Dip it in a vat of salted melted butter. Enjoy.

💸PRICE: $4.00 USD per corn
