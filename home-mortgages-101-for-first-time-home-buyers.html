This video is the ULTIMATE GUIDE to home mortgages.

A mortgage is one of the biggest and longest-term loans that a household will ever take on, so it makes sense to understand it inside and out.

1. The 4 factors that make up a home mortgage
2. The 7 different types of mortgages
3. A live demo through my home affordability spreadsheet
