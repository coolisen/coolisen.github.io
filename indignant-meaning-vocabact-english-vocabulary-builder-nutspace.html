Meaning of Indignant
adjective		 [ɪnˈdɪɡnənt]
feeling or showing anger or annoyance at what is perceived as unfair treatment.
-
Indignant in a sentence:
Rohini became rather indignant on being asked to return the speakers which according to her she never borrowed.
