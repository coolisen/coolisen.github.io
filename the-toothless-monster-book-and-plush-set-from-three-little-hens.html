Today TTPM is reviewing The Toothless Monster from Three Little Hens. When a child loses a tooth, place it under the plush monster’s tongue. The Tooth Fairy will take it and give it to the monster so he can eat tingtang fruit! Parents take a plastic tooth and insert it into the plush monster’s mouth for kids to find the next morning. A storybook accompanies the soft plush. See more of how it works in this video review. For full review and shopping info► https://ttpm.com/p/29402/three-little-hens/the-toothless-monster/?ref=yt

Product Info: Getting a visit from the Tooth Fairy might be exciting, but it’s not as fun as cuddling up to The Toothless Monster. This plush and book set offers an alternative to the traditional Tooth Fairy experience.
