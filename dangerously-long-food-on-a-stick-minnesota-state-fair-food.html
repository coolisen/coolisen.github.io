EVERYTHING ON A STICK AT THE MN STATE FAIR

1. BIG FAT BACON
WEBSITE: https://bigfatbacon.com/		 IG: @bigfatbacon

🥓BACON ON A STICK: Take a slice of bacon on a stick, place it in the oven for 25 minutes, bring to the stovetop to crispify it then baste it with maple syrup and a blend of seven peppercorns, sea salt seasoning and serve! Top with chocolate, orange chipotle, or other toppings.

💸PRICE: $5.00 USD

💸PRICE: $7.75 USD

💸PRICE: $7.00 USD
