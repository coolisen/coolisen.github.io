DESCRIPTION
In this excerpt from "How To Paint Creatures - Sculpey Painting Techniques," master FX artist Casey Love (Predators, The Thing, AVP: Requiem) shares his approach to detail airbrushing for small-scale creature busts and monster maquettes.

You’ll learn how to subtly accentuate wrinkles with paint and highlight sculptural details with color, as well as how to airbrush ultra-fine veins to add a layer of interest (and life) to your character creations.
