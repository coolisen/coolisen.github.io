INTERACTIVE ENGLISH GRAMMAR
The aim of these lessons is to allow teachers to drill basic grammar in interesting and realistic ways.
These are not self-study materials.
They have no audio content.
They are designed for teachers, whose role is to elicit basic grammar-focused controlled dialogue.
The lessons should be used to supplement other coursework (reading, writing, listening etc.).
The lessons are designed to be used for group teaching in classrooms with an interactive whiteboard, however they can easily be adapted for use in other contexts (individual lessons using an iPad, for example).
