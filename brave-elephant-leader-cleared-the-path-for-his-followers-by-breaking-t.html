The story of intelligent leader who paved the way for his followers by breaking the electric fence. He smartly broke the electric fence only using his tusks.
With increase in human population density and change in land use patterns, elephant habitat is being continuously reduced. As a result, elephants come in to closer contact with human resident areas in search of food.
This adventurous herd of elephant found accidently during their journey in search of food. They were leaded by a brave majestic tusker. They had to face an unexpected obstacle; an electric fence. Electric fences are erected by the villagers to protect their cultivations being raided by the elephants.
But the intelligent leader in the herd, bravely started breaking the electric fence, only using his tusks. It’s amazing! The smart elephant was able to break the fence. As a result, all his followers in the herd were able to cross the village border escaping the electric fence barrier.
