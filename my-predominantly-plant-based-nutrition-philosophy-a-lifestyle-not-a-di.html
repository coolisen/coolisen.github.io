About a year ago, I transformed my life with the adoption of my "Predominantly Plant-Based" nutrition philosophy.

Predominantly Plant-Based is a way of eating that takes into account the scientific health benefits of a plant-based diet AND the psychological/physical benefits of intuitive eating.
