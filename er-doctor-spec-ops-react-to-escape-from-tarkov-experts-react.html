Cameron and Jordan (what a combo!) are with Gamology today reviewing the much asked for: Escape From Tarkov. Follow them as they both re-explore the game but with a different perspective together examining how both their jobs intertwine in dangerous situations. And remember we all have friendly bacteria on our skin so be nice to it!

In Escape from Tarkov, two private military companies (United Security "USEC" and Battle Encounter Assault Regiment "BEAR") are engaged in combat in the fictional Norvinsk region. As a result of political scandals and corporate collapses, Tarkov has become a mess and warring factions have turned the city into a shell of its former self with sections of the city being controlled by aggressive locals called "scavs".﻿
