Baha’i Blog’s “Studio Sessions” is an initiative where we invite Baha’is and their friends from around the world to come into a studio and share the Baha’i Writings put to music.

In this Baha’i Blog Studio Session, a nice group of youth who call themselves ‘JYSEP Goodna’ sing “At All Times”, from the Writings of Abdu’l-Baha.

You can also download the audio version of this song from Baha’i Blog’s Soundcloud page here:

https://soundcloud.com/bahai-blog/at-all-times-by-jysep-gooda

This song was filmed and recorded in Brisbane, Australia, February 2016.

Here is the quotation used for the lyrics below, followed by the quote with a link to the chords.

"At all times do I speak of you and call you to mind. I pray unto the Lord, and with tears I implore Him to rain down all these blessings upon you, and gladden your hearts, and make blissful your souls, and grant you exceeding joy and heavenly delights…" - Abdu’l-Baha

——

Chords: D Bm G D
