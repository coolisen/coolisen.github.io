In Ottawa, NDP MP Charlie Angus calls on Prime Minister Justin Trudeau to take four concrete steps towards reconciliation. Trudeau’s office says he called Tk’emlúps te Secwépemc Chief Rosanne Casimir over the weekend and apologized for not accepting two invitations to attend the B.C. First Nation’s event in Kamloops on September 30. More than 200 unmarked graves were discovered at the site of a former residential school in that community. The prime minister took part in a ceremony on Parliament Hill on the eve of the new federal statutory holiday.
