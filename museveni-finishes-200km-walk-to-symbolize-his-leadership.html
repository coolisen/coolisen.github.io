Uganda’s President Yoweri Museveni has finished a 200km trek. The walk is meant to symbolise his leadership journey since his days as a guerilla leader in the country’s jungles. During the trek, Museveni promoted his revolutionary ideas and wealth creation goals. But critics are viewing the march as little more than a publicity stunt ahead of next year’s elections. CGTN’s Hillary Ayesiga reports.
