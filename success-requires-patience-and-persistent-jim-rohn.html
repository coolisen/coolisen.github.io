Jim Rohn talks about 2 qualities that increase your chances of success: Patience and Persistent. Patience means being able to handle the passing of time. While, persistence means, keep on working towards your goal despite all hardships and challenges. Both patience and persistent are required for success. Impatience is the biggest enemy of Ambition - Jim Rohn
