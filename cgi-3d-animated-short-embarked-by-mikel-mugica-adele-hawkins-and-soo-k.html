Check out this adorable and heart-warming 3D Animated Short film called "Embarked" about a treehouse and the kids that love it, created by the talented team of Mikel Mugica, Adele Hawkins, and Soo Kyung Kang! Produced at Ringling College of Art and Design. For more information, please see the details and links below:

Production of this film took 3 semesters, from January 2014 till May 2015 and the programs used were Photoshop, Autodesk Maya for rigging, modeling and animating, Zbrush was used for modeling too, and they used Pixar´s Renderman for shading and HDR lighting Studio for Lighting.
