Over the course of Sunday, June 13th director James Mangold of among other things Logan and Ford vs Ferrari fame; and soon to be Indiana Jones 5 fame, went into a Twitter meltdown.
He is not the first director to do that, so we actually do have some historic precedence for this – and if history is anything to go by, this is something that will haunt and taint the movie from now, all the way up to its release, and beyond.
In this editorial, @AndreEinherjar will begin by briefly going through the Mangold situation, and then compare it to other past director meltdowns and what followed from them; such as Paul Feig’s Ghostbusters, Josh Trank’s Fantastic Four, and even Kathleen Kennedy and Rian Johnson’s Star Wars The Last Jedi.
