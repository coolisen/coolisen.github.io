Introducing the NEW Nikon Z9! Jake, along with professional photographers Jen Pottheiser and Joe McNally, try out Nikon’s first mirrorless flagship camera. The incredibly fast and reliable performance is due to the brand new, full frame 45.7MP BSI Stacked CMOS Sensor and new Expeed 7 processor. You’ll never miss a moment because of the 20 frames per second shooting at full resolution with a buffer of over 1000 frames and no blackout. Other amazing features include 8K video and 4K120p video internally, an updated auto focus system with 493 focus points, 3D tracking with subject detection, dust and moisture sealing, integrated vertical grip, sensor shield and more!
