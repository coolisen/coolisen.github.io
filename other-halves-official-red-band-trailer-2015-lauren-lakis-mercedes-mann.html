Other Halves is a techno-horror thriller about a team of programmers who develop a revolutionary new dating app. On the night before the app is set to launch, they discover it causes strange side effects: users lose all self-control, becoming amoral, lascivious, violent… evil. They consider shutting the app down, but…

Evil is profitable.
