IELTS Speaking Practice: Shopping

Shops like this, the local butcher’s and the local baker’s are still extremely popular in Spain, whereas in the UK, they tend to have been swallowed up by the big supermarkets. The reason I am out here this morning is because today’s topic is all about SHOPPING. What you like and don’ t like about shopping. We are going to be looking at useful language, vocabulary and expressions you might need to talk confidently on that subject.
