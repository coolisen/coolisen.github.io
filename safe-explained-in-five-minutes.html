This video explains SAFe 5.0 in five minutes (and 29 seconds). It illustrates how the SAFe methodology uses cadences, ceremonies, and processes applicable to teams, programs, large solutions, and portfolios. About Scaled Agile, Inc.
The Scaled Agile Framework® (SAFe®), created by Scaled Agile, Inc., is the #1 framework for business agility, with proven benefits in productivity, time-to-market, quality, and employee engagement.
