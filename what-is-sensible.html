SensiBLE is - a low power, small form factor (20x30mm), ready-to-use system-on-board equipped with 3-axis accelerometer, 3-axis magnetometer, 3-axis digital gyroscope, pressure, relative humidity, ambient light and temperature sensors.
Have I mentioned microphone already? Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) 4.1, and Low Power ARM® 32-bit Cortex®-M4 CPU with FPU are on-board as well.
