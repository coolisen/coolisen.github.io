3 Tips for answering… “WHY SHOULD WE PROMOTE YOU?”
PROMOTION INTERVIEW TIP #1 – Give a passionate answer that shows you genuinely want the promoted position.

PROMOTION INTERVIEW TIP #2 – Tell the interview panel you have the necessary skills, the qualities, and the experience to excel within the promoted position!

PROMOTION INTERVIEW TIP #3 – Show that you CARE about their organization and explain how you intend to stay working for them for a long time into the future.
