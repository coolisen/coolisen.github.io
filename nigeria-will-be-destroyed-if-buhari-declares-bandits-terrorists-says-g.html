Islamic Cleric, #SheikhAhmadGumi has condemned the push to designate bandits as terrorists.

The Sheikh on Monday wrote a post on his Facebook page titled: Declaring bandits as terrorists will come with a prize!

He said if President #MuhammaduBuhari declares bandits as terrorists, it will mark the end of Nigeria as a sovereign and united entity.
