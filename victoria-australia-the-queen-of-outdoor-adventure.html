Explore the Grampians in Victoria, Australia – the Queen of outdoor adventure. Take in the sites with Lonely Planet, as we head out on spectacular walking tracks leading to dazzling waterfalls, wildlife in the wild, awe-inspiring lookouts and indigenous rock art dating back over 30,000 years. Savour celebrated local wine at cellar doors and sublime dining options with a side of adventure via rock climbing with the experts from Absolute Outdoors.

• Reed’s Lookout, Grampians National Park

• Rock Climbing with Absolute Outdoors, Grampians National Park
