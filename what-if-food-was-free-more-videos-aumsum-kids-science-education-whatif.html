What if Food was Free?

Fourthly, if Food was Free, chefs may not get paid well anymore, they may audition for a new job.
Fifthly, if Food was Free, restaurants may not remain profitable anymore, they may start charging people entry fees.
Lastly, if Food was Free, there may not be much requirement of money anymore, some people may quit their jobs.
