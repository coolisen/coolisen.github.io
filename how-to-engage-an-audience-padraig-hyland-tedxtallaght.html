Padraig Hyland is an audience engagement specialist and works with people to help them engage and excite audiences around their ideas. He has worked with TEDx Tallaght as a speaker coach for a number of years and his passion is helping people find their authentic voice. His company The Core Story has developed a unique process to enable business to communicate with an audience. As well an extensive international business career he has also worked a professional Stand-up Comedian.
