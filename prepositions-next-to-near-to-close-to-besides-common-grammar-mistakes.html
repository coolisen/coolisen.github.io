English prepositions always confuse students and the most common grammar mistakes are made with English prepositions. Students are often perplexed with, which would be the correct preposition to use in a sentence. A commonly confused set of prepositions of place - Next to, Near to, Close To, Besides. I have noticed people use them interchangeably. In this English grammar lesson, learn how to use these prepositions correctly with making any mistakes.
