In this video, Stefan talks about the power of anticipation and how to handle problems before they arise.

Things happen in life that are outside of your control, related to the economy, personal finances, relationships, health, work, etc.

Stefan shares with you Tony Robbins’, "7 Triggers of Business Crisis". These triggers reveal that change is inevitable in life and in business. Change is the ONLY constant.

In order to be successful, you need to anticipate these changes, instead of reacting to them.
