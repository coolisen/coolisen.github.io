But on the flip side:
Firstly, face mask factories will start popping up all around the world.

Thirdly, with face masks compulsory, lipstick and lip balm industry will probably face gloomy times.
Fourthly, dating with face masks will probably be awkward to start with, but people will slowly but surely get used to it.
Lastly, with face masks compulsory and beards and moustaches no longer visible, men will probably stop caring whether to shave or not.
On a serious note, people will have to clean their face masks regularly else the entire process will be futile.
