Trust and doubt, risk and reward, faith and fear; every believer, who has walked with God, knows what it’s like to feel conflict in the heart, mind, and spirit. But the conflicting voices within you can be shut down when you fight for what’s rightfully yours! Fight for a peaceful Spirit that resolves the internal arguments inside of you. Fight to forgive the friends who let you down. Fight for the taste of the bountiful life God promised you! Fight because you are worth fighting for!
