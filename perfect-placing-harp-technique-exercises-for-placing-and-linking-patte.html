PERFECT PLACING is a collection of 15 harp technique exercises that focus on placing your fingers in groups and linking groups together into short patterns. A great fit for elementary to intermediate levels and written to be playable on smaller harps with bass C as the lowest string.

Watch the harp lesson tutorial to preview pages from the book and to discover creative ways to build musicianship skills with "Perfect Placing".
