The Background

On a fall day outside of Philadelphia Dan was bit by a mosquito–something you don’t really think much about. But 24 hours later Dan was in the hospital in serious condition, he was diagnosed with West Nile Virus and Meningitis. From that moment forward our lives were changed forever. Unable to work for over a year we were financially devastated.

Patti, a high school teacher, did anything she could just to make ends meet, but it never seemed to be enough. With seemingly nowhere to turn we watched the movie “The Secret”. That lead us to a new mindset, changing our life yet again. With a new mindset we built programs to empower others through positive thinking and built a marketing team of over 100,000 members.

The Present

Now living in the era of Covid we have created a program that will inspire and help people around the world. Our mission:
“Inspire others to live their lives, by experiencing our journey as seen through the lens of our camera.”
“Exploring Through Our Lens” –an RV Adventure is a program that will not only tell people that their dreams are possible, but show people that anything is possible with the right mindset.

The Dream

We are currently in the process of selling everything we own (except our computers and cameras equipment), buy an RV, to take to the road and live our dream of seeing America and spending quality time with our children and 9 grandchildren that live on both coasts.




The YouTube Channel

“Exploring Through Our Lens” an RV Adventure has started as a Youtube Channel exploring the wonders of America, giving the viewer an inside look at the people and places that are right in their backyard.

The TV Show

We have a relationship with an international TV and Film distributer, The Global Genesis Group, in the first 6 month of the journey “Exploring Through Our Lens” an RV Adventure, we will shoot and edit a 44 minute pilot for a TV show to sell to the networks.

The Documentary

The last segment of our adventure will have us interviewing and cataloging interesting people all over the country with the intent of telling their stories. We will then cut those stories into a documentary, The Backbone of America (working title). We believe these stories will be powerful for our viewers.

The Hunt’s

Dan & Patti have been married for a little over 12 years. Patti, a high school teacher for 20 years, became an international speaker and trainer. Inspiring and educating others to live their dream.
Dan has worked in the entertainment industry for most of his life; actor, stuntman, producer and director. He even taught a film class at Villanova University, creating a film that was in contention for an Academy Award for a documentary he directed.

After Dan’s illness, we took a chance and changed everything quitting our jobs and starting our own company. The mission at the time, which is still our core mission, is to help others before they get in the situation they found themselves just a few years before. They have traveled the world speaking to crowds from 100s to 1,000s of people, always with the message of positive thinking.

We are that couple that does everything together. From shopping to cleaning and everything in-between. Working together can be challenging at times but if you have ever seen the two of us on stage you would see the magic we create.
The Vision

“Exploring Through Our Lens” an RV Adventure has been a dream of Dan’s for years and now the timing just seems right. 2020 has changed the world for all of us, and this project will show people that there is more than Social Media and Zoom calls. We have already started our Youtube channel and are going on day trips in the Las Vegas area to get the ball rolling. We plan to take to the road on March 1, 2021 with the intention of traveling for at least 1 year.

America has so much to offer, from the beauty of our state and national parks to the diverse cities and regions of the country. There are so many festivals and interesting events and the stories of the people that make America the greatest country in the world.

“Exploring Through Our Lens” an RV Adventure will not only tell the stories but give the viewer the experience of everything and everyone we encounter on our travels.

With the power of positive messaging, the backdrop of America, and the people “Exploring Through Our Lens” an RV Adventure will inspire others to take that camping trip, go to the beach or any other adventure they have dreamed of.
