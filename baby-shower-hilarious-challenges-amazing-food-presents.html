My decorations were absolutely perfect and unlike anything I have ever had at a party in my life–honestly prettier than my wedding!! I’m so thankful for family and friends who came and for family and friends who helped with my very special day. I also want to thank people who travelled quite far in order to set up decorations and food at my house!!
