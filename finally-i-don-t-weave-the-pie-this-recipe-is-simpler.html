Ingredients:
dough:
flour - 1 kg (2.2 lb)
water - 400 ml (13.5 fl oz)
milk - 200 ml (6.76 fl oz)
egg whites - 1 piece

sugar - 10 g (0.35 oz)
yeast - 10 g (0.35 oz)
To leaven 60 min

yolk - 1 piece
