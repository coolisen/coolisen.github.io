Hello everyone and welcome back to Black Desert Online.

Today we’ll be talking about farming and why you should know the basics and maybe have a farm of your own!

Farming is easy to get into if you’re able to support the upfront contribution point cost, however, in my opinion, it is a must if you’re into cooking or alchemy and doing the imperial crafting deliveries of these two life skills.
Farming is also a good money making life skill later down the road. I’m at Professional 3 and I’m able to get 4.5M\day if I sold my produce. But I know you can easily get 20M+\day with certain rare crops.
It’s a life skill that you can do hands off or hands on making it great to do as you’reworking or busy with something else. Sometimes I’ll be griding with my main fighter and I’ll swap to my life skiller just to tend to my
crops and then change back to grind some more!
