Deputies in Alabama raced to save a 1-year-old who allegedly was overdosing on fentanyl. Bodycam video shows the response. When a Jefferson County Sheriff’s deputy finds the little girl sprawled out on the floor, he tries to look for a pulse. He administered Narcan, a nasal spray to revive people overdosing on opioids. It worked to wake the child up, but she was then rushed to the hospital. Inside Edition Digital’s Mara Montalbano has more.
