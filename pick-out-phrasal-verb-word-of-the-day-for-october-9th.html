Pick out (Phrasal verb)

Meaning:-to choose, find, or recognize something or someone in a group:

Examples:- 1. From all the puppies, we picked out the smallest one to take home.
2. Could you help me to pick out the yellow pieces?
3. Let’s pick out the bad potatoes from the basket.
