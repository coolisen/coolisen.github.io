Join me for a day in the life of a doctor in clinic! Today I’m working in the general internal medicine rapid assessment clinic and you will get to meet other members of the team - occupational therapist (Famie), pharmacist (Alice), a junior resident (Wendy) and our staff physician (Dr. Panju). We will be working together as a team to see patients who were sent from the emergency department or family doctor’s office for a specialists opinion.

ALSO you will get to see what it’s like when doctors talk to each other… the real medical jargon!! Let me know what you think about that haha!

THANK YOU so much to the whole team for being involved in this video! It is such a pleasure to work with such a supportive team of health care providers who are motivated to provide public education!
