For me, fog (mist) is a wonderful natural spectacle. The changing light conditions and the calm create a soothing and peaceful mood. Here, the world still seems to be in order and time flies by in a flash.

Those who already know me a little and have followed some of my videos may know my video "The beauty of a rainy day", in which I tried to capture the wonderful atmosphere of a foggy rainy day against the gorgeous backdrop of Carinthia in so-called "bad" weather. And what happens when fog hits an urban area like my adopted hometown Frankfurt am Main? Then even for me, although I already know Frankfurt so well and have seen so much here, suddenly completely new and beautiful impressions arise.

I am happy to present you with this video my second collaboration with Daniel Haussmann from Haussmann Visuals: "Frankfurt am Main in the mist 4“K - let yourself be enchanted by wonderful shots that give Frankfurt a magical atmosphere. And maybe you’ll feel the same as I did: when I created the video and combined Daniel’s shots with the music, I got goose bumps a time or two.

Daniel Haussmann is a professional photographer, drone pilot and filmmaker from the Rhine-Main area with an impressive repertoire of great shots. If you want to learn more about him, just have a look here:
