This is an introductory video clip for BIHO that combines a 30mm self-propelled anti-aircraft weapon BIHO with surface-to-air missile KP-SAM.

BIHO is reported to have received the best evaluation result in India’s on-site test evaluation last year, and is currently awaiting to be selected as the preferred bidder by the Indian government.
