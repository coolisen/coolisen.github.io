Embrace, one of the most successful UK bands of the 90s & 00s, are set to reissue their chart-topping albums, ‘Out Of Nothing’ and ‘This New Day’, for the first time on 180g vinyl on 30 October via Craft Recordings. The long-sort-after reissues were cut at London’s Metropolis Studios & will come with free download codes and printed inner sleeves. Pre-order is available now https://embrace.lnk.to/OONTNDLP

To mark the two landmark album reissues, Embrace will play an exclusive one-time-only online live performance of ‘Out Of Nothing’ in full. Tickets are going on sale here: https://link.dice.fm/MPWvShSdd9
