Video 4 in our series on safer injecting. In this video, we demonstrate the steps to do a drug injection.

This 5-part video series presents vein care tips for people who use injection drugs. Using a harm reduction approach, we talk about the planning, supplies, setup, and aftercare for safer injecting. Specific topics in this video include drug preparation, injection site selection, hygiene, tourniquet use, vein care, vein trauma, and safer injection practices.
