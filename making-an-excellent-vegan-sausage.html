Making a sausage of plant-based raw materials only is one of the holy grails in vega production. For any craftsman sausagemaker, it sounds pretty difficult. We use natural whole foods only, based on grains and legumes and at a low level of processing. We do not use high pressure or high temperatures in our processes (no extrusion), which means a low use of fossil fuel in our processes.
