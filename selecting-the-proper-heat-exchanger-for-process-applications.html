When it comes to process applications, heat exchangers aren’t one size fits all. With a wide selection of heat exchanger types available, how do you know which one is the best for your process requirements? You need to carefully consider many factors, including the type of application, the operating conditions, the properties of the fluids being handled, and any space constraints.

In this webinar, you’ll learn the criteria used to determine the best heat exchanger for the job, as well as the advantages of selecting one design over another. We’ll also introduce you to specialized heat exchangers for especially difficult process applications.
