Since you clicked on this video, you are probably here to discover the difference between SINCE and THEREFORE. :)

SINCE and THEREFORE are very useful linking words that we use in everyday English, the key difference between them is that one of them is used to introduce the REASON and the other one is used to introduce the RESULT of an action.
