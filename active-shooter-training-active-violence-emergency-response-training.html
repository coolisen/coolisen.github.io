With AVERT, we go beyond active shooter training. Our training empowers people to save lives through strategies that help individuals anticipate potential danger, react quickly and become an immediate responder to life-threatening injuries and bleeding.

Our active shooter training is both for individuals and groups. Whether you are looking to become an instructor or request on-site training for your company, school, church or other group, AVERT has the experience to prepare your organization to react and respond in life-threatening situations.

When active shooter or active violence situations occur, seconds count. You cannot always wait for
security, police or EMS to arrive and our training will enable you to make rapid survival decisions during an emergency. Learn more online at: https://www.get-avert.com/
