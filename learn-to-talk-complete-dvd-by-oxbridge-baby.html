‘Learn to Talk, is a unique DVD that uses a combination of sounds, music, visual stimulation and basic sign language to introduce your little one to a variety of vocabulary as they learn to say their first words.

Created in conjunction with Gillian Hart, Paediatric Speech & Language Therapist, the DVD works on a number of levels to stimulate development rather than simply being a passive experience. These unique qualities mean that children can watch ‘Learn to Talk’ again and again and get something new out of it each time.
