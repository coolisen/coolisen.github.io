The official audio of "Happy W U" by Arthur Nery feat. Jason Dakhal

R&B and soul artist Arthur Nery releases his much-anticipated single of the year, "Happy w u." This new track is made even more special with the participation of fellow R&B artist, Jason Dhakal. These two emerging artists in the scene create a captivating and sweet mid-tempo track imbued with Arthur Nery’s remarkable music style.

A love so simple yet so affectionate that it brings pure bliss — that’s what "Happy w u" is all about. Derived from personal experiences, the song spells out how can also be effortless that despite all the hurdles you face, you’re still able to receive the same amount of love you give.
