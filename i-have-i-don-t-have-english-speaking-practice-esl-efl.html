Hello Everyone! In this video, you will learn to use "but" in example sentences. You will use positives statements and negative statements. In the second have of this video, you will practice speaking English on your own.

Language in use:
I have a tennis racket but I don’t have tennis balls.
I have a bat but I don’t have a baseball.
I have paper but I don’t have a pencil.
I have my books but I don’t have my lunch.
I have my drink but I don’t have my sandwich.
I have a red dress but I don’t have a yellow one.
I have a dog but I don’t have a cat.
I have a daughter but I don’t have a son.


This video is designed for students, teachers and anyone wanting to learn English.

My videos are vocabulary-based for conversation practice. Each video is themed to provide context for learning. To insure success, every video is designed with open slots for vocabulary substitution practice. These patterns allow students to practice on their own and teachers can have their class practice together as a group.

These videos also work great for icebreakers and class discussions.
