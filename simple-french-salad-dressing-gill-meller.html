Delightfully simple salad dressing - make it once and have it in the fridge for two weeks.

INGREDIENTS:

- 2 tsps dijon mustard
- 2 tsps unrefined caster / granular sugar
- 2 dessert spoons cider vinegar
- salt and pepper
- bashed clove of garlic
- 100ml light sunflower oil
- 100ml extra virgin olive oil
