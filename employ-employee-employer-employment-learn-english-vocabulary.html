In this video, learn how to use Employ, Employee, Employer, Employment. These confusing English words are all used to talk about being paid to do work, but when and how they’re used is slightly different.

Your choice depends on sentence structure, parts of speech and who you’re specifically talking about…the one doing the work or the one paying for the work to be done.

By the end of this lesson, feel confident using: Employ, Employee, Employer, Employment.
