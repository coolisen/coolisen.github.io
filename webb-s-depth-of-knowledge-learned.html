This video provide an understanding of how Webbs depth of knowledge is different than blooms taxonomy. It compares the two on similarities and differences. But going back to Webbs depth of knowledge, this video is intended to help educators utilize it in the classroom to guide instruction and increase student achievement. This lesson also explore the different knowledge levels of Webb’s. Knowledge level 1, recall and reproduction, is explored through examples. Each of the knowledge levels, recall and reproduction, skills and concepts, strategic thinking, and extended thinking are all described with examples. The video differentiates all of the different knowledge levels of Webb’s depth of knowledge.
