This support video is designed for BJU Press’s Reading 2 video course. Mrs. Walker explains sustained silent reading—the practice of providing students with extended, uninterrupted time to read. Sustained silent reading helps children by teaching them wise reading choices, increased concentration, new vocabulary, a positive attitude, and introduces recreational reading.
