My kids can eat a dozen of those! Low carb seaweed chicken breast rolls. Also great option for option for school lunch!
Ingredients:
1 lb Grounded chicken breast
1/3 cup cooked corn
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbsp mung bean starch (or corn starch)
1/4 tsp black pepper powder
1/2 tsp mushroom bouillon powder
2 sheets of seaweed
