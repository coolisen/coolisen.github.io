The first 10 minutes are strictly focused on inner thighs. The last 5min are dedicated to the outer thighs. And no, it will not give you hip dips :D

The next day, my thighs were soooo sore, that I was barely able to finish the workout when filming :D :D :D Look closely, my legs are SHAKING!!
My brother Dennis was behind the camera and motivated me with "don’t you give up during a Pamela Reif workout. If somebody can do it, it’s Pamela Reif. Hold your leg up!"



If you need breaks - TAKE THEM! Don’t worry too much about that. You will improve over time :) that’s the best feeling! The video is in full length which means you can just follow whatever I’m doing 30s for each exercise.
