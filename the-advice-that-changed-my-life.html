When I joined my first job after MBA, I was given something that I hated: Accounting.
As a result, I did a shoddy job of presenting the financials.
For a few days, my manager used to correct my errors.
After some time, he taught me a life lesson that has stayed with me till date!
I can’t begin to tell you how much that life lesson has helped me, and how much it has helped everyone whom I have downloaded it to.
It was a not-wanted yet much-needed lesson on trust.
As I share with you that lesson, I also share 3 ways you can cultivate trust in your own life - at work, in relationships, everywhere!
After all, trust is the emotion that has been running human beings for so many centuries! :)
I hope this life lesson helped you as much as it has helped me in the last 15 years, and if you do find it useful, please share it with as many people as you can, so that all of them are also benefited :)
