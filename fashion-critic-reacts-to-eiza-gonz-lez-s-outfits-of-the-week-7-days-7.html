Eiza González is a Mexican actress and singer who has been building up her public profile for some time, and with this Vogue outfits of the week video, she attempts to only further push herself into good graces of the fashion elite. She has already worn established brands like Versace, Gucci, & Dior, while also becoming a favorite for Louis Vuitton and getting a fragrance campaign from the top luxury brand. As we can see throughout the video brands like Burberry & Tom Ford are up her alley, but she also likes to uplift younger designers like Del Core, Khaite, Luar, Sandra Weil, & Adam Selman which we love to see as well!
