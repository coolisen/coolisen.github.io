How do you use your 12 hours of sunlight? At PUB, we harness it to generate clean energy by deploying solar panels on the rooftops of our plants and on our reservoirs, to power our water facilities. With the recent completion of our 60MWp floating solar farm on Tengeh Reservoir, one of the world’s largest, our local water treatment plants will now be 100% green. And that’s how we reduce our carbon footprint.

As we do our part to fight climate change, we’ll continue exploring ways to harness clean energy sources. Watch the film and tell us how you can contribute to a more sustainable future.
