ART IDEAS FOR KIDS AND PARENTS

Art is very good for kids. Painting helps to relax, get rid of stress, concentrate, and last but not the least, it helps to develop fine motor skills and creativity. Today, we collected the best art ideas for kids and I think you should try them all and tell us what you liked most.

First of all, use your creativity when it comes to painting. Be creative in everything – did you know that you can paint on egg trays? Seriously, you can use it as a cool canvas for your masterpiece. Check out our cool 3D incredible painting and try to repeat it.

Paint together with your kids – this cool activity is awesome for families. Your kids can prepare a cool sketch, and you can brighten it up with watercolors. Play as a team and create more and more awesome masterpieces together.

Cool paintings can be created without paint – you can use grated wax crayons and make a cool picture suing them. Another extra creative way to paint – painting with yarn. Ask your parents to help you with hammering and go ahead with creative painting.

Don’t forget about upcycling when it comes to art. Toilet paper rolls can be used as a material for cool and elegant wall décor. Check out what we did.

You can preserve your memories in style by using our awesome idea – use metal lids as magnet frames for your pictures. You can also make frames out of cardboard and wrapping paper with a nice print.
