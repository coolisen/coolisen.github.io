Watch Casper the White Lion lying right next to the road causing a massive traffic jam as everybody wants to see this world famous White Lion in Kruger National Park.
Casper is one of only three White Lions currently alive in the wild.
The other two are a male and a female from the Birmingham pride in the Ngala Private Game Reserve adjacent to Kruger Park.
White Lions are NOT albinos and are also not a different species of lion. Their condition is known as ‘leucism,’ a rarity where a recessive mutation in the gene causes the lion’s coat to vary from near-white to blonde, rather than the common tawny.
Filmed just South of Satara Rest-camp on the H1-3 close to the N’wanetsi River bridge.
