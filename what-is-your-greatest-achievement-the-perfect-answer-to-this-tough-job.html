2 TIPS FOR ANSWERING THE INTERVIEW QUESTION: What is your Greatest Achievement?
JOB INTERVIEW TIP #1. Where possible, give an achievement that is related to your work.

JOB INTERVIEW TIP #2. Use the STAR technique to structure your answer:

TASK - Outline the task that you needed to do.
ACTION - Go into detail about the action you took.
