What is a reputable breeder?
The best way to locate a responsible breeder is to contact the breed organizations or kennel clubs in your area. … Reputable breeders, sometimes dubbed “hobby breeders,” do not breed puppies for a profit. They do it for the betterment of the breed, for the love of dogs, and… well… for the fun of it.
