How To Change iPhone Payment Method

Update a payment method
Tap the payment method, then edit your information. Make sure that your information matches what’s on file with your financial institution. You might need to enter the security code that’s printed on your credit or debit card. If you see Apple Pay after the last 4 digits of the card number, edit your billing information in the Wallet app.

Add a payment method
Tap Add Payment Method. You might see a suggestion to add a card that you use with Apple Pay. (If you add a new credit or debit card, you might see an option to add the new card to Apple Pay.)

Remove or reorder
iPhone showing the Manage Payments screen with the remove button next to the credit cards.

Remove a payment method
Tap Edit, then tap the Remove icon or swipe left and tap delete.

Change the order of your payment methods
Tap Edit, then use the move order icon to rearrange your payment methods. Apple will attempt to charge payment methods in order from top to bottom. You can’t change the order in which Apple ID balance is charged.
