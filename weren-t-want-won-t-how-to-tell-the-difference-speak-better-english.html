Weren’t Want Won’t - learn the difference between all 3 and improve your English Accent

These 3 words can be very difficult to tell the difference between. Won’t and want in particular are often confused and require specific training to tell the difference. If you want to learn english pronunciation and the correct british accent for weren’t want won’t, this video will show you how to do so.

The ‘er’ sound in weren’t is a long vowel sound. Wan’t is a short vowel sound that is pronounced with your lips rounded. ‘Won’t pronunciation is normally the hardest to learn correctly because it is a dipthong and is made of 2 English vowel sounds.

In this video I give you an easy exercise that you can practice straight away to immediately improve your pronunciation of all 3 words:
