I just want to point out all the benefit you get out of this weapon; Final blows increases your rate of fire, after hitting 3 separate enemies you gain 35% more damage for 10 seconds, every final blows reloads 1 ammo to the weapon, smoothbore increases the spread and range. Finally Heavy Handed will refund 1 ammo for every final blows while being surrounded.

I’m really happy this weapon dropped for me and I’ll be keeping an eye for the Adept version when it comes around.
