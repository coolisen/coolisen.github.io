As you know, Jimmy is much more than just a beloved TV talk show host, he is also a respected TV Judge. For the past 35 years, he has been ruling on cases with the help of his trusty bailiff Guillermo and after a long break from the law, they are back in the courtroom. These are real cases with actual litigants who for some reason decided to put their legal fate in the hands of “Judge James.” In this case, the plaintiff Tiffany is suing her hair stylist Marlene for $1200 claiming that a haircut was so bad she was forced to stay home for days. Since Tiffany had shown a photo of Meagan Good as an example of what she wanted, we invited Meagan to the courtroom to help with the ruling.
