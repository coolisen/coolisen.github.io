A first-person perspective festive London store tour inside Liberty London Department Store, taking a look at The Tree of Liberty - a spectacular bonsai Christmas tree, suspended from above as to appear floating, made from blue velvet branches and real reindeer moss dyed pink. The tree was inspired by the Christmas Liberty print Jeweltopia, which draws inspiration from an ancient Tree of Life design and features lush foliage, exotic animals and festive treasures.
