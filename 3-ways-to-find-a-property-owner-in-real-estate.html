How do you find the owner of a property? How do you make a lead out of a real estate property owner? Why would you want to find out who the owns a property?

Finding the property owner will put you in contact with the person who owns a piece of real estate. By doing so, you can set up a meeting with the property owner to determine if they are interested in selling their property or buying a new property.

This is an effective way to finding a new client in real estate. In this video, Robert Rico shares with you 3 ways you can find the owner of a property.
