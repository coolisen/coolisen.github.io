2011 is a busy year!

JPL is preparing to launch four NASA missions: ocean-sensing Aquarius, Jupiter-bound Juno, the Gravity Recovery and Interior Laboratory (GRAIL) twin moon orbiters and the Mars Science Laboratory rover, Curiosity.
