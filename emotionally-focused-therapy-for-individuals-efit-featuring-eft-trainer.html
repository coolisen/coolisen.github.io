Emotionally Focused Therapy is the gold standard of couples counseling, and the same interventions and methods are just as effective in helping individual clients as well. Many EFT therapists that are used to working with couples may forget that they already know how to work with individuals to a degree, and they already use the principles and interventions when working with a particular partner within the coupleship.

Join We Heart Therapy Host Anabelle LMFT, Certified EFT Therapist and EFT Trainer/Supervisor Lorrie Brubacher LMFT as they discuss how to use EFT with Individual clients.
