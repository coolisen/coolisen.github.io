Tube & Berger release their first EP on Suara after two magnifique remixes for Pleausekraft & Belocca and AFFKT in 2012. "Pleasure Dip" is also their first record after "Lovebreak", the tune who reached #1 Overall Beatport Top100 and after 4 months is still on the Deep House Top100. The German combo-star have worked for many months exclusively in this ep, trying to get the right inspiration. The got it. "In My Dip" and "Pleasure Of Pleasure" keep the line drawed from Tube & Berger on 2012: emotive beats for the dancefloor. Two well planned music melodic masterpieces which will get tons of plays in 2013.
