The weather was fine in most of the country. But in the north of Japan, it snowed along the coast of the Sea of Japan, and in some areas there was a strong destructive hail. This caused great problems with traffic, and also forced the local utilities to throw forces to clear snow from streets and courtyards, as well as roofs of buildings.
