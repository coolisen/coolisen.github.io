More About " The Edge":
In a close future, in the heart of a forest in the North where reigns anarchy, the young Hawa and her father survive as many refugees. It was during a theft of food, that the Hawa’s life switches. Shes loses what she cares the most and launches a journey to find the thieves up to the edge of this forest.
