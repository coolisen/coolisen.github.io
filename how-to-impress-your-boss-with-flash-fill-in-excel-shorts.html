Here is a simple way to impress your boss! Instead of wasting time typing in data in Excel manually, use Flash Fill! Start typing your values and once Excel recognizes a pattern it will fill the rest of the values automatically for you. Save time without writing formulas! You can easily split your data in Excel with Flash Fill. No need to write formulas and type these out manually.
