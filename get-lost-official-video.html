“I’ve learned the most about who I am in the times I’ve gotten ‘lost’ from my path and the expectations of others. There have been many times, whether around substance, getting divorced, or coming out, where folks have told me that I’ve, ‘lost my way’ - and they hope that I will, ‘find my way back.’ So this song is really about that experience - a kind of response for anyone who’s ever been scolded for discovering their own expression.” - David Wimbish
