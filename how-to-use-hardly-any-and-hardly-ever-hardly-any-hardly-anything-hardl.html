In this lesson, we are going to have a look at how to use hardly any and hardly ever.

0:28 HOW TO USE HARDLY ANY / HARDLY ANYONE / HARDLY ANYWHERE / HARDLY ANYTHING
We use hardly any, hardly anyone, hardly anything and hardly anywhere to mean not many or very few.
he has got hardly any money
EXAMPLES:
The test was so hard hardly anyone passed.

I want to travel i’ve been hardly anywhere.

My date was horrible she hardly said anything the whole time.

1:51 HOW TO USE HARDLY EVER
We use hardly ever to mean almost never.
EXAMPLES:
I hardly ever make coffee at home. I almost always go to a cafe.

My parents are fat they hardly ever exercise.

The eight o’clock bus is hardly ever on time

I don’t like my glasses I hardly ever wear them.
