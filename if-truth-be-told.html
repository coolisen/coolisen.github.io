If Truth Be Told (A Monk’s Memoir) is Om Swami’s memoir slated to be released in November 2014 by Harper Collins India.
Often when you do things in the world whether that is getting educated, getting married and having kids, ultimately all of that is done so that you can be happy but this does not lead to real happiness. This only leads to some temporary pleasures. Every time you have a glimpse of that pleasure you mistake it for happiness.

Self-realization is that state of transcending these temporary pleasures and trading them for constant state of happiness. All of our pleasures live in the mind anyway. Imagine if you could go to the source of those pleasures, the mind itself.
