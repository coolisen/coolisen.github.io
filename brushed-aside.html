Mark Lahood created this incredible film for me as a way to share my passion for makeup in a creative & visual way.

Brushed aside represents how I felt & treated when consistently playing with the idea of pursuing my interest in the art of makeup.

For years I listened to others and would let them tell me who I ‘should be’.

There was no denying my connection to the industry once I delved in. I finally found myself & who I am.

This is my short story.
