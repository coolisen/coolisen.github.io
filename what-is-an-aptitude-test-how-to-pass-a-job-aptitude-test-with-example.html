Numerical reasoning test questions. 01:37
A numerical test assesses your ability to work with numbers and numerical data quickly and efficiently.

Verbal reasoning test questions. 05:11
Assess your ability to use the English language to create sentences, choose words, and determine facts based on information presented within the aptitude test question.

Diagrammatic reasoning test questions. 06:41
This type of aptitude test assesses your ability to interpret patterns, shapes, and diagrams to solve simple problems.
