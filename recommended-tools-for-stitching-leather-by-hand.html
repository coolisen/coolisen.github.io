Hello everyone! The purpose of this video is to introduce you to some essential and recommended tools that I prefer to use when stitching leather by hand. In the video I try to briefly cover the function of each tool and it price points. There are additional stitching tools and machines available beyond what is demonstrated in this video. The tools in this video are meant to cover the basics needs of hand stitching and what I discovered to be essential for hand stitching. Below is a list of tools demonstrated in this video:

1. Stitching horse/pony
2. Polished stone board
3. Plastic poundo board
4. mallet
5. stitching chisels
6. wing divider
7. Stitching groover
8. Stitching stylus
9. Stitching awl
10. hand stitching hole pliers
11. scratch awl
12. blunt needles
