Croatia / Town of Sinj - rich tradition and excellent gastronomy



For travel packages on this destination, make sure to visit http://www.pointerstraveldmc.com



The hinterland of Split Riviera is the continental part of Dalmatia. It consists of towns located mostly on rivers with
extremely rich ancient and old Croatian heritage. Sinj is a town in Croatia, which belongs to the Split-Dalmatia County. It is the center of the Cetina region. The total area is 194.27 km2. Sinj and its surroundings have 24,832 inhabitants, while the town itself has 11,448 inhabitants. Sinj is recognizable in Croatia and beyond by the Sinj Alka, a knight’s game that has been held in Sinj since the beginning of the 18th century, as a sign of victory over the Turkish invaders. A small, dynamic place with good traffic connections that will make you feel welcomed and relaxed. The city of exceptional tradition, rich cultural and natural heritage, and excellent gastronomy.

In this part of the hinterland of the Riviera, you can experience a peaceful and primeval vacation.
