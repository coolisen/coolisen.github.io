The AGC Technovation Centre of AGC Glass Europe brings together some 250 researchers, technicians and engineers in brand-new premises focused on improving the glassmaking processes and developing new glass products for the construction and automotive industries. Designed as a technological showcase for R&D achievements, the AGC Technovation Centre is destined to become the worldwide centre of excellence in architectural glass for the AGC group.
