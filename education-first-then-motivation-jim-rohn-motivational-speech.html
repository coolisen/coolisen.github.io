It is the investment in knowledge which pays the best interest. However as Jim Rohn says knowledge accumulated in itself yields no results, you have to let your learning lead t o action. Moreover, education is better than motivation, as if you are motivated, yet do not possess the necessary skills, you will be unable to work on your newfound motivation and it will pass. Therefore, educate yourself daily to reap the benefits of life.
