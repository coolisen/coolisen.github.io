How often do you hear an unfamiliar word or get lost when someone is speaking?

At that moment, you have a choice. You can stay silent and lost the opportunity to participate in the conversation and share your thoughts.

Or you can use simple clarifying questions to stay fully connected and successfully navigate the conversation.

That’s exactly what you’ll learn to do with 13 ways to clarify in English.
