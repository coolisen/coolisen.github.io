Fastwalkers exposes the shocking truth about UFOs and Extraterrestrials, a truth that has been suppressed and hidden for centuries. The facility tracks hundreds of these "bogeys" each year from a subterranean facility deep inside Colorado’s Cheyenne Mountain. See and hear the people that the governments of the world never wanted you to meet.
