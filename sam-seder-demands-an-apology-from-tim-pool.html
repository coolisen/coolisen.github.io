In October of 2020, Tim Pool invited Sam Seder to debate him. Sam happily accepted. Then Tim Pool started mentioning travel and accommodation plans, and Sam stated the obvious: No one was traveling or coming into contact with other people because there was a global pandemic. Tim Pool thought that Sam Seder was being a "coward" and spoke ill of him "canceling" their debate. Now Tim has Covid-19 and it’s a whole thing because he called Joe Rogan about Ivermectin. Sam’s concerned with what’s really important, though: Why hasn’t Tim apologized to him yet? Clearly he was correct to be cautious.
