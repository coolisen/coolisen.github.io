This fantasy diorama took 5 weeks to make! It is 87x87 cm and consists of fantasy elements inspired by Lord of the Rings, Wheel of Time, etc. How many references can you find on the sign-post?

__________

- Prefabricated miniature trees and foliage
- Static grass
- Resin and various miniature water texture supplies
- Resin printed miniatures
- Hot glue flames
