A MAN whose acne medication gave him a severe medical condition is now embracing his body by modelling. Kirt from Louisiana, USA went to a dermatologist in 2019 and was prescribed a drug for his acne. Unfortunately Kirt had a severe reaction to the medication, and it gave him a condition called D.R.E.S.S syndrome (drug rash with eosinophilia and systemic symptoms). Within five months, the condition caused him to develop alopecia and vitiligo across his entire body. Despite this, Kirt has found a new love for his body by modelling and raising awareness for his condition.
