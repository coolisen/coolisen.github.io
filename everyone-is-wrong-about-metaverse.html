WHAT IS THE METAVERSE?
Andrew Steinwold has a great quote where he said Crypto is the money of the metaverse, NFTs are the “stuff” in the metaverse that we bring with us, DAOs or decentralized autonomous organizations are companies in the Metaverse and tokens are rewards for participating in something social.

HOW DO I INVEST IN METAVERSE?
The question is - will there be one meta verse that we settle on? Will it be Facebook’s? Will it be the Sandbox? Decentraland? The VeVeVerse? Or will there be many? Is it a universe or is it a multiverse? It’ll be all of it.

The Metaverse represents life, liberty and property as written by John Locke in 1869 on the human identity and self hood. The MetaVerse is our reinvention or simulation for the control over life, liberty, and property through a world that is governed by code as law.

LIBERTY:
Liberty is our identity in terms of who we want ourselves to be. Liberty gives us the freedom to interpret ourselves in any way we want in the Metaverse because we’re not bound by physical law, we can bend our digital universe and become any character we choose.

PROPERTY:
Bitcoin is not really a cryptocurrency - it’s not life, it’s not liberty - it’s property. Property that we truly own and control. These assets give us control by way of decentralization over the value we accumulate throughout the course of our lives without the theft of that value from a government debasing our money.

Ethereum is a digitization of contracts so that we don’t need courts to interpret laws for us because our laws would be governed by smart contracts - not by human opinion.

LIFE:
Where do we choose to live and express this life? We can’t choose to live this life in the real world because we are bound by physical and social laws in place that prevent all of this from being this way. So we have to simulate this utopia in the digital world. That’s what the Metaverse truly is. It’s not a place - it’s a time when those principles of life liberty and property become real.

WHAT DO I INVEST IN AGAIN?
NFTs are going to play a big part of displaying those flexes and collectibles which is why I’ve started investing in them and making them a part of my digital identity. Bitcoin and Ethreum are going to be the "money" of that future world. Money will always be valuable - they are the foundations of liberty and property.
