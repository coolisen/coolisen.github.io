Alex Gaudino began his adventure in the music business in Italy in 1993 at the famous Flying Records and UMM. In 1998 with the support of Giacomo Maiolini, owner of the legendary TIME Records, Alex became a&r of Rise Records, which soon became one of the most respected European labels, signing hit records like The Tamperer and Black Legend. "Destination Calabria" is the single that elevated Alex to the level of international top djs as Eric Prydz and Fedde le Grand – it reached Number 1 in the European Dance charts in May 2007 and has already achieved the following national chart positions - No.1 in Belgium, No.4 in UK, No.6 in France, No. 10 in Holland
