Watson Duke needs to account for the financial state of the Public Services Association (PSA).

So says former President of the Association, Jennifer Baptiste-Primus.

Mrs. Baptiste-Primus says the Finance Committee of the PSA, and the General Council needs to hold Mr. Duke to account for his actions.

She adds that in the 12 year period of Mr. Duke as President of the PSA, the conference of delegates has only met once in 2012, therefore Mr. Duke has no mandate to spend the membership’s funds.

Mrs. Baptiste-Primus was a guest on TTT’s Now Morning Show on Monday.
