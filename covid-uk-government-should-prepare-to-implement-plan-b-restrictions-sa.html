Boris Johnson says all measures are under review, but urged people to get their booster jab.

In the last 24 hours, there have been just over 49,000 new Covid cases in the UK, and 180 further deaths were reported.

While 5.3 million booster doses of the vaccine have now been delivered across the UK.
