Charles W Fluharty who was 57-years-old when he went missing, was found in his car underwater, solving a 7-year-old missing person case in East Liverpool, Ohio October 8, 2021.

Fluharty was last seen in East Liverpool, OH on 10/19/2014. He was an amputee, wheelchair-bound, and left behind clues that he would not be returning that evening.

Charles was seen getting into his car at his apartment and he hasn’t been seen since.

This 3-part series documents Adventures With Purpose’s search alongside Detective Greg Smith, and Jacob’s Team from Chaos Divers (http://bit.ly/SubChaosDivers), following the clues Charles left behind, leading to the discovery of where Charles and his car had gone missing in the Ohio River these past 7-years.
