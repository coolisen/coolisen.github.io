Pfizer’s "Value of Medicines" papers outline the social health and economic value of a variety of classes of treatments. The supporting 90-second video illustrates the positive impact that medicines have had on a patient, Matt Woods. Matt faced a life-threatening health condition. His story provides insights into the important role that medicines have played in his journey towards improved health.
