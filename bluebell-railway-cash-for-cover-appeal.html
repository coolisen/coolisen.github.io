Update - March 2015: We’re still fundraising - we’ve raised £366,000, enough for the basic shed and roof, but if we get to £385,000 by September, we will be able to afford the roof supports and section of the two-storey building for the additional facilities needed. Many thanks for all your support so far! - http://www.bluebell-railway.co.uk/bluebell/trust/appeal/about.html

Operation Undercover Phase 4 - the Horsted Keynes carriage shed extension. If we can raise £125,000 to match the £125,000 promised by sponsors by mid-November (The Double Donations Dash - DDD125), we can get a roof over 10 carriages. If we raise another £100,000 on top of that, we’ll have enough money to complete the roof over all 20 carriages.
