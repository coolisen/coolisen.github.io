The first 10 minutes are strictly focused on inner thighs. The last 5min are dedicated to the outer thighs. And no, it will not give you hip dips :D

My brother Dennis was behind the camera and motivated me with "don’t you give up during a Pamela Reif workout. If somebody can do it, it’s Pamela Reif. Hold your leg up!"
