An activist who has helped repeatedly bring traffic on the M25 to a standstill in protest at the Government’s slow progress in insulating homes has suggested he does not "particularly care" about the cause.

In a bizarre radio exchange, Liam Norton, one of the leaders of the Insulate Britain campaign, admitted he had failed to insulate his own flat in London, saying – apparently flippantly – "because I’m a hypocrite".
