We kickoff season 8 of "The Pulse" as the Aggies welcome back a full capacity crowd into Kyle Field when they took on the Kent State Golden Flashes.

Texas A&M Football’s three-time EMMY award-winning weekly show, ‘The Pulse,’ gives fans an all-access pass into the Texas A&M football program with highlights from the previous week’s game as well as going inside the locker room, training room, practice fields and class rooms from the viewpoint of the student-athletes and coaches. The 12th Man Productions staff also delivers personal features that include visits to the players’ hangouts and hometowns for a truly insider experience.

Visit 12thman.com/thepulse for more.
