I rode the 2021 DGR in London, starting at Old Street roundabout, before meeting the rest of this year’s London Distinguished Gentleman’s Ride riders at Bike Shed in Shoreditch, and then riding over to Richmond Park, before returning for lunch at Bike Shed.

A total of 9.5 hours riding around London- a fantastic day with great people and an incredible selection of motorcycles!

The Distinguished Gentleman’s Ride raises money for prostate cancer research and men’s mental health. If you’ve never ridden in a DGR before, I highly recommend it- really great vibes all around!

If you’d like to donate, my DGR account is here: https://gfolk.me/dobbs
