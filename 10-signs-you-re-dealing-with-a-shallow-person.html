What are the sign’s someone’s superficial? And how do you know if you’re dealing with a shallow person? Well, we’ve all come across shallow people at one time or another. A person like this just seems to lack depth, as they are overly concerned about superficial things - mainly appearance.

People like this often have narcissistic tendencies. Their self centeredness is quite evident in their behavior. Grandiosity, arrogance, jealousy, and manipulation are common traits.

Consider this list as a checklist to determine whether you or someone you associate with is a superficial or shallow person.
