Edmonton Girl Guides are going door to door for their annual Hike for Plenty food drive to collect food for those in need. Watch for a flyer notice at your door and please leave food donations on your front porch before 10 am on March 7th. The Guides will be coming by between 10 am and noon. You can also text FEEDYEG to 20222 to make a monetary donation of either $10 or $20.
