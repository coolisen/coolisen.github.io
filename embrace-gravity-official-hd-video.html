Embrace, one of the most successful UK bands of the 90s & 00s, are set to reissue their chart-topping albums, ‘Out Of Nothing’ and ‘This New Day’, for the first time on 180g vinyl on 30 October via Craft Recordings. To mark the two landmark album reissues, Embrace will play an exclusive one-time-only online live performance of ‘Out Of Nothing’ in full. Tickets are going on sale here: https://link.dice.fm/MPWvShSdd9
