The United Nations Volunteers (UNV) programme is the UN organization that promotes volunteerism to support peace and development worldwide. We believe volunteerism can transform the pace and nature of development and it benefits both society at large and the individual volunteer. UNV contributes to peace and development by advocating for volunteerism globally, encouraging partners to integrate volunteerism into development programming, and mobilizing volunteers.
