What is Strategy? This video covers why defining strategy is a difficult task, reviews traditional but inadequate definitions, synonyms, and the etymology of strategy. Finally, the video discusses a clear and actionable definition of strategy. ⬇️

You can pick up a copy of HBR’s On Strategy here: https://amzn.to/2FWwJBx — it’s a must-read for students of strategy.
