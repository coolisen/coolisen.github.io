Adam Knight is a singer/songwriter raised on the backroads of Southern Oregon where he grew up listening to Soul, R&B and Acoustic music. Adam is a versatile performer equally singing Pop, Neo Soul, EDM, R&B and Acoustic genres. Known for his hit "Ive Got The Gold", which has been streamed more than 30 Million times worldwide, while charting in countries like Germany, France and Turkey.
