The disc was following the NATO ships. Those who saw the disc were numerous. And there were also many other UFO sightings in Western Europe on that same day during the NATO exercise. One of these sightings was a flying disc, seemingly metallic, at 7.30 pm over the most important military airfield in Denmark.
