My decorations were absolutely perfect and unlike anything I have ever had at a party in my life–honestly prettier than my wedding!! I’m so thankful for family and friends who came and for family and friends who helped with my very special day. I also want to thank people who travelled quite far in order to set up decorations and food at my house!! I know it took one person over 2 hours, but we REALLY appreciate your amazing decorating talents. We also appreciate overnight shipping to get some of the decorations to our house that we put up ourselves.
