We are excited to bring to you our suite of Dental Assisting education modules with web-based skills simulations. A first for dental education! This video is only a tiny sneak peek into our Dental Assisting - Assist with Composite Restoration module. Each SIMTICS module doesn’t only have a video, but also has simulations where you can learn and practice the procedure interactively, step by step, then test your skills.
