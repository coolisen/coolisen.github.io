Some twins are so similar that people find them hard to tell apart… and twins Dolly and Daisy, 25, have taken this to a new extreme. They have spent around £140,000 on various cosmetic surgeries to look as ‘identical’ as possible. They even love getting surgery done together - by the same surgeon, on the same day. We meet Dolly and Daisy who tell us why they have become addicted to altering their looks and mum Christina, who has never had any procedures, and worries about the health implications on her daughters.
