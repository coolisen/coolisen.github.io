Fastwalkers exposes the shocking truth about UFOs and Extraterrestrials, a truth that has been suppressed and hidden for centuries. Fastwalker is the NORAD (North American Air Defense Command) code word used to classify unidentified flying objects (UFO’s) approaching Earth from outer space and entering our atmosphere. The facility tracks hundreds of these "bogeys" each year from a subterranean facility deep inside Colorado’s Cheyenne Mountain. See and hear the people that the governments of the world never wanted you to meet.
