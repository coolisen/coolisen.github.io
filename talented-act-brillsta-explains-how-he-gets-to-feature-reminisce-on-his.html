Fast rising Nigerian artiste, #Brillsta has gave us an insight on how he was able to have Reminisce on his hit single "Bullion Van" Remix.

While speaking in an exclusive interview on Naijaloaded TV, Me Myself And I show, Brillsta took us on a very touchy journey of his growing up and how he still managed to keep the Musical journey alive despite the whole pressure to quit.

Brillsta who recently released a brand new single titled "Bullion Van" Remix gave us the breakdown of how he and his team was able to get Baba Hafusa on the song, and the seamless it was working with him on the song.
