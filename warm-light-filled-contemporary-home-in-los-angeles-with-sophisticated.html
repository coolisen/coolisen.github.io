Escape the ordinary. Warm light-filled contemporary home boasting a sophisticated palette of natural materials & landscape design. Tranquil living in the famed Beverly Grove area. Dramatic, expansive floor plan lends itself to a wealth of natural light & towering ceilings with custom skylights.
