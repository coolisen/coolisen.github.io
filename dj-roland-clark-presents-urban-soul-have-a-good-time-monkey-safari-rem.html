House producer / lyrical ace / DJ Roland Clark is here once more with a couple of tunes mixed like never before. One of his most renowned songs "My Urban Soul" gets a truly dope-ass funky deep re-rub by Brooklyn producers Walker & Royce. Best known for their work on Nervous, Glasgow Underground, Crosstown Rebels and others, they took this previous #1 Billboard Club hit and ran it through the underground ringer. So good! A bonus cut on this double release is the more recent "Have A Good Time," which will prep any room ready to rock. German deep house production team Monkey Safari is on remix duty offering more of that deep funky goodness. House a la mode all the way.
