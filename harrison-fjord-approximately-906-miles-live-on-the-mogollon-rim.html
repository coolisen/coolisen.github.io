Harrison Fjord perform live on the edge of a cliff at the Mogollon Rim. After months of planning, a team comprised of 20+ people captured the live performance on a particularly remote cliffside with the use of 5 cameras, 2 generators, glass prisms, a mixing deck, and an aerial drone.
