Join us as we head to The Heath in Knutsford for the annual May Day Fun Fair! In this action packed vlog we share on ride footage from many of the different machines on offer at this awesome fair. This includes Extreme, Techno Power, Mega Spin and much more! We also ride an awesome travelling junior coaster, take a walk through a Bavarian themed fun house and much more!
