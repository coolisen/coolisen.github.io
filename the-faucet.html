In this video, Dan Bailey drops by Austin Malleolo’s gym for a quick photoshoot. Malleolo, who doesn’t want to miss a chance to work on fitness with his friend while he’s in town, writes an impromptu workout.

In a nod to Bailey’s notorious drooling problem, Malleolo calls the workout “The Faucet”: 20 minutes of rope climbs, D-ball over-the-shoulder throws and burpee box jumps.
