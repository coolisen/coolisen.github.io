Here is a glimpse into a typical day of my life as a trader! My routine as a Trader comprises of waking up at 9:00am EST, setting my coffee to brew at 9:05am EST and then heading to my desk to open my Trading platform to set up my charts. I then log into the Trading room and start scanning for stocks that are gapping up or down so I can build a watchlist of stocks I want to day trade. I then post my list in the Trading room by 9:15am EST. Afterwards, I take about 15 minutes to review my open portfolio positions and other swing trades I might be in.

I day trade until about 11:00am EST and then I hop onto Live Stream in the chatroom where I review all my trades for the day. I also scan for some options trades and answer questions from the chatroom members while giving them feedback on their Trades. Typically, I am done by around 12:00pm EST. I usually spend the rest of my day in meetings or other daily activities. Other days, I might still be at my desk scanning and looking for swing trades.
