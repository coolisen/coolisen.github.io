Hot on the heels of their releases on Defected and Flamingo, Dutch duo Roul and Doors make their debut on the label of Hardwell alongside Defected Records favorite Franky Rizardo for their collaboration called ‘Elements’. ‘Elements’ showcases a refreshing and powerful fusion of Rizardo’s tribalesque beats and Roul and Doors’ latin/progressive sounds and powerful synths. With the label flag bearer Hardwell and young studio partner Dannic on tough yet uplifting remix duties, this all-out Dutch invasion marks another knock-down track from Revealed Recordings. This release is sure to make a sizeable impact on dance floors across the globe.

Elements will be out November 12 on Revealed Recordings
