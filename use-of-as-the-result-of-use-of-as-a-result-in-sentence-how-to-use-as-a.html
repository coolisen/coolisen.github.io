So let’s get started

(Zakhamo ke nateejay mein uski mout hogayi.)
He died as a result of his injuries.

(Woh haadsay ke nateejay mein zakhmi howa hai.)
He is injured as a result of an accident.


(Yeh selaab bahhar ki taiz baarisho ke nateejay mein aaya.)

(Aag ki waja se woh khaufzadah thi.)
She was scared as a result of the fire.
