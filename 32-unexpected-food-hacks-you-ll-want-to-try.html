Since all of us are spending more and more time in the kitchen these days, I wanted to share with you some interesting and cheap recipes you can try today.

You can also use silicone mold to make DIY chocolate-coated ice-cream. First, add some vanilla ice-cream in your silicone mold and then freeze it. After that, take the ice-cream out with some popsicle sticks, dip it in melted chocolate sauce and Bon appetite.

If someone adores white chocolate, you can gift them a milk container made of white chocolate and also fill it up with m&ms and other candy.

If you’re having friends and family over and you want to prepare something special for them. Simply, add some melted chocolate in an ice tray, and then add some nuts or coconut flakes as a filling. Then, add some more melted chocolate on top to make amazing treats.

I also show you how to make tasty granola bars with oats and other sweet candy.
