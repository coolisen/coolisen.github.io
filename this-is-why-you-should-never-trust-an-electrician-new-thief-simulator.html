Welcome to American Theft 80s. American Theft 80s is an upcoming game developed by the same team behind Thief Simulator and today I’m checking out the massive demo for the game to see what it’s all about. In American Theft 80s, you go around town doing odd jobs for shady individuals to earn money to obtain more impressive thieving tools to get into even the most secure of homes and businesses.
