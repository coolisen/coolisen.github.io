🌹Scared to raise your hand in class? Hate getting called out by your teacher? Wondering what to do if you’re shy in class? I’VE BEEN THERE! SO whether you’re in online class or in-person class, I’ll be sharing 5 TIPS I used that you can use too, in order to overcome your shyness and truly break out of your shell… and finally learn how to feel comfortable participating in class🌹
