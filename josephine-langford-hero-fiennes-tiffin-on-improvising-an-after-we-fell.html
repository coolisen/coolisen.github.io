Josephine Langford and Hero Fiennes Tiffin spoke to THR all about the third installment in the ‘After’ franchise, ‘After We Fell.’ The stars who play Tessa and Hardin opened up about filming in Bulgaria and revealed their favorite memories from the set. They also spoke about the scene they are most proud of, as well as revealed that one "Hessa" scene in the film was completely improvised. Plus, they opened up about Tessa and Hardin’s evolution as characters throughout the series, as well as what they’re most excited for fans to see in this new movie. ‘After We Fell’ is available on digital Oct. 19.
