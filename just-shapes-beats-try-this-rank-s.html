The final part is completely random and pretty dang hard to pull off without hits. One trick to work around the randomness a bit though is to use the quick restart fuction on the pause screen (only available in playlist). If you use quick restart the level will restart with the same random configurations so you can kind of anticipate the patterns on later attempts.
