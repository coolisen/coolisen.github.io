U.S. Travel Association’s IPW will reunite the world in 2021 in Las Vegas, providing an in-person platform for the global travel industry to rebuild and renew our relationships around the world. As the leading international inbound travel trade show, IPW will be a model for restarting international travel and trade shows safely, with the health of all attendees as our top priority.

Past IPWs have generated more than $5.5 billion in future travel by connecting U.S. travel exhibitors with travel buyers and media to promote their product and negotiate future business, securing America’s position as a foremost global travel destination and showcasing the best of what the U.S. has to offer.

To register for IPW 2021 visit: https://www.ipw.com
