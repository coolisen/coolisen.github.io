Dr. Jordan Rogers continues preaching through The Book of Genesis in this sermon from Genesis 39:1-23 entitled, "The Prevailing Purpose of God."

Psalm 33:11 says, “The counsel of the LORD stands forever, the plans of His heart to all generations.”

The Lord’s purpose will always prevail! This is clearly seen in the life of Joseph, who was subjected to various and awful circumstances so that, by the Lord’s design, he would rise up in the house of Pharaoh and save the nation of Israel from destruction due to worldwide famine in those days. We can, and should, take great comfort in fact that neither hardship, persecution, nor dire circumstances can derail the purpose of God!

The following is a sermon from Genesis 39:1-23 and below you will find the video recording and a sermon outline to help you follow along as you watch. I hope this study tool helps you grow in your love for the Lord Jesus and in your obedience to Him.

Sermon Title: “The Prevailing Purpose of God”

Text: Genesis 39:1-23

Sermon Summary: The LORD’s purpose will always prevail!

Sermon Structure: 3 truths concerning God’s prevailing purpose

vs.1-6a—Joseph’s Enslavement

vs.6b-18—Joseph’s Harassment

vs.19-23—Joseph’s Imprisonment

Truth #1: Hardship will not derail the purpose of God. (vs.1-6a)

Truth #2: Persecution will not derail the purpose of God. (vs.6b-18)

Truth #3: Dire circumstances will not derail the purpose of God. (vs.19-23)
