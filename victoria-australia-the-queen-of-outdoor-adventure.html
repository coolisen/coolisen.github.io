Explore the Grampians in Victoria, Australia – the Queen of outdoor adventure. Savour celebrated local wine at cellar doors and sublime dining options with a side of adventure via rock climbing with the experts from Absolute Outdoors.

• Rock Climbing with Absolute Outdoors, Grampians National Park
