This is Episode 1/2 of my 2019 BBC Radio 4 series ‘Rhys James Isn’t’.

The original blurb read: ‘Comedian Rhys James doesn’t have any opinions. Over two episodes he’ll be trying some on, from both sides of the argument, to see how they fit.’

Essentially I spoke on the same subjects from different perspectives over two episodes. A left wing one and a right wing one.

This show was broadcast in December 2019. People were sort of talking about BBC balance but it wasn’t the endless dredge of a chat it is now. Anyway, enjoy part 1. Part 2 is on my channel if you want to hear the other side. I recommend listening to both.
