Ray St. Germain was born in 1940 in a small town just outside of Winnipeg, Manitoba. He is a legendary country music performer who was inducted into the Canadian Country Music Hall of Fame in 2010. He is a proud Metis and family man. His five children are all musically inclined and some of them have appeared with him on the "Big Sky Country" television show from which most of these video clips were recorded back in the 80’s. His vocal skills are impressive and if you find his voice to be a little bit reminiscent of one Elvis Presley you shouldn’t be surprised since he was once known as Winnipeg’s Elvis.
