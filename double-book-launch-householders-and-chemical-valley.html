You’re invited to the double book launch of two exciting Biblioasis books: Kate Cayley’s HOUSEHOLDERS and David Huebert’s CHEMICAL VALLEY! Co-hosted by Another Story Bookshop (Toronto, ON) and Bookmark (Halifax, NS). We’ll be streaming live on Facebook & on YouTube. There will be readings, discussions, an audience Q&A, and a book giveaway. You could win a copy of one of the new books!
