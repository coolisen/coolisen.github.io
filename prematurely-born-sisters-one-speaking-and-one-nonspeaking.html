Maggie was born weighing 11 ounces at 25 weeks. After being given a 0% chance to survive by medical staff she would eventually go home after 154 days in the NICU. Maggie’s twin brother Noah did not survive the difficult pregnancy. Katie, her younger sister, was also born prematurely at 27 weeks and spent 112 days in the NICU. Katie adores everything about her nonspeaking sister Maggie and considers them both to be miracles.
