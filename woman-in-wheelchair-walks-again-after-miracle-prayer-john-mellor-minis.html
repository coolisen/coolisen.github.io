Samantha had been unable to walk unaided for more than two years and relied on a wheelchair for a year. She was born with a deformity in her legs and bones and suffered fractures and excruciating pain in her fibula, making her legs too weak and painful to carry her weight. However, after prayer in Jesus’ name in Bendigo, pain left, strength increased and she was able to get out of her wheelchair and walk independently. She testified the next morning that she walked a long distance and felt herself getting stronger.
