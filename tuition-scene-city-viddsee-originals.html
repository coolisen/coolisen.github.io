Attending tuition class after school is a common experience for most. What goes behind the closed doors in these private tuition classes? Does the end justify the means for these tutors if they are producing good results?

13 year old Alan attends his tuition class full of dread and fear as he faced a tutor whose behaviour is too extreme - her methods of teaching are aggressive and borders on physical aggression. Will he be able to defend himself by gathering enough evidence of abuse to expose her? Tuition is a haunting tale about a young boy challenging an authoritative figure in his life to stand up for his own beliefs and values. The film also highlights societal issues - do we put too much emphasis & pressure on good grades… & turn a blind eye on other issues?
