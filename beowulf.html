A timeless epic tale of heroism and triumph, the legendary Old English tale of Beowulf is brought to life by Academy Award®-winning director Robert Zemeckis (The Polar Express, Cast Away) with digital rendering and cutting-edge performance capture technology. With a script by Oscar® winner Roger Avary and graphic novelist Neil Gaiman, one of the world’s oldest heroic sagas is reimagined as never before with the world’s newest technology.
