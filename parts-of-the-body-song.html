Use this Body Parts Song for learning about the Parts of the Body, it can be such a fun experience, especially when you can learn them while singing and moving! This Parts of the Body Song teaches the following body parts: Head, Eyes, Nose, Ears, Mouth, Neck, Shoulders, Stomach, Arms, Elbows, Hands, Fingers, Legs, Knees, Feet, and Toes.
