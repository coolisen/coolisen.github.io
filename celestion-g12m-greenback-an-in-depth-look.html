The Celestion Greenback is one of the most iconic speakers. Starting life in the 1960’s as the first speaker to have a ceramic magnet it soon became a favourite of Marshall, Vox and many more. This helped cement its place in music history.

Over the years it has changed slightly. This, the classic series M greenback is the standard model, not to be confused with the Heritage series M (watch out for our videos on this).

In this video we use our Fatbaby and Compact 1x12 cabs (specs below).
