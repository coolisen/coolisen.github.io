BIZARRE BITES AT THE STATE FAIR OF TEXAS

1. RUTH’S TAMALE HOUSE
INTERVIEW: Ruth Hauntz

BRISKET BRITTLE: It’s peanut brittle, except without the peanuts and with brisket instead.

💸PRICE: $12

RUTH’S STUFFED FRIED TACO CONE: Take a fried corn tortilla cone and add two scoops of cilantro rice. Add a scoop of black beans, then perfectly cooked barbacoa. Add a scoop of pico de gallo and sprinkle with cheese. Drizzle with jalapeño sauce. Top it off with a lime wedge!

💸PRICE: $18

2. SCRUMBSCIOUS PIESHAKES
ADDRESS: 450 FM 548 Suite 112, Forney, TX 75126
SOCIAL: @scrumbsciouspieshakes
WEBSITE: https://scrumbsciousburgers.site/
INTERVIEW: Chris Howard

TEXAS EASTER EGG: In a mixing bowl, add cream cheese, add mixed cheddar cheese, add brisket, add bacon, add green chili, add jalapeño. Add Tex mex seasoning. Mix it all together. Roll into an egg shape. Dip it in egg yolk and roll it in bread crumbs with Italian seasoning. Deep fry. Decorate with Easter Egg colored ranch dressing.

💸PRICE: $25

3. PIZZA, FUNNEL CAKE, LEMONADE

FUNNEL CAKE BACON QUESO BURGER: Two funnel cake "buns" are fried to a golden crunch and topped with powdered sugar. Then they’re placed on either side of a freshly-grilled burger patty, crispy bacon, and gooey queso.

💸PRICE: $13

4. VANDALAY INDUSTRIES
SOCIAL: https://www.facebook.com/VandalayInd/
INTERVIEW: Abel Gonzales

FRIED COKE: Fried Coke or Deep Fried Soda is a frozen Coca-Cola-flavored batter that is deep-fried and then topped with Coca-Cola syrup, whipped cream, cinnamon sugar, and a strawberry slice.

💸PRICE: $8

FRIED BUTTER: To create these ooey gooey bits, a cube of butter is rolled into dough, making sure it is in the center, forming a perfect dough ball home for the butter. Then they’re deep fried and topped with honey and cinnamon sugar.

💸PRICE: $8

5. GOURMET ROYALE
SOCIAL: https://www.facebook.com/gourmetroyalellc/
INTERVIEW: Greg Parish

DEEP FRIED SEAFOOD GUMBO BALLS: These deep fried seafood gumbo balls are loaded with chicken, shrimp, crab meat and andouille sausage, rolled in a saltine cracker and breadcrumb batter and then deep fried. They’re served with a side of gumbo roux sauce, fried okra, saltine crackers and hot sauce.

💸PRICE: $25
