Quick directions on how to use the rechargeable e-cigar.

The Rechargeable E-Cigar box contains:
-(2) Pre-charged e-cigar batteries
-(3) Cartomizers
-(1) Wall charging adapter

To use the Rechargeable E-Cigar:

Take a battery out of the box. Batteries come charged.

To assemble the e-cigar take a cartomizer out of the box and plastic packaging. Remove the rubber plugs on both ends of the cartomizer. Line up the USB ports on the battery and cartomizer and insert them together.

You can puff like you would a traditional cigarette and you’ll see the tip glow red as you inhale.

Continue drawing the e-cigar at your own rate and enjoy.

Once the flavor and vapor diminish it’s time for a new cartomizer.

To replace the cartomizer:

Pull off the used cartomizer and dispose.

Pull a new cartomizer out of the box

Remove the rubber plugs

Insert the cartomizer onto the battery.

To charge the battery:

To charge the battery, separate the battery and cartomizer.

Insert the battery into a computer usb port or a the wall adaptor that came with the unit.

The ash end will stay red when it’s charging and will go off when it is fully charged. Batteries take about 2 hours to fully charge.

Insert a cartomizer back onto the battery.

Puff and enjoy!
