A teacher at the Arlington, Texas, school posted a photo of his classroom door barricaded with a bookshelf and desks. It all reportedly started when a fight broke out between students at Timberview High School. Four people were injured, and the armed student fled school grounds in a car, but was apprehended shortly after.
