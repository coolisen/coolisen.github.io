You might be very surprised indeed, if we told you that Apple has decided to create its own car, the Icar, which is fully autonomous and electric! So are we already at the dawn of a new era, where we would see smartphone or computer manufacturers launching into mass production of cars? And will we really be able to see Apple cars driving our roads tomorrow, with their train of applications and IOS updates?
