As a Latina in tech, Eliana Murillo isn’t just paving the way for young women hoping to break into the industry — she’s also making big waves for an entire cultural community, too. Between a prominent position as Google’s head of multicultural marketing and serving as the CMO of family-owned Tequila Alquimia, her plate is full with constant work and a packed travel schedule, but she wouldn’t have it any other way.
