In a vision, I am overshadowed by the feathers of wings.

People of God, continue to be living testimonies of God’s love, to a faithless generation who has succumbed to darkness.

People of God, pray for their conversion. The beast is entering into the holy place where his reign will begin.

Disturbances in the heavens intensify and continue to cause the earth to shake and crack.

A great purification is taking place because of man’s rebellion. People of God, pray, and remain faithful.

I stand ready to defend you from the wickedness and snares of the devil.

Remain strong in the Lord, Jesus Christ, Whose coming is very near.
