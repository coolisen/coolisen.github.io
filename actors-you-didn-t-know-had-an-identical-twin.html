When we see actors on a screen, it’s easy to think of them as products, or persons without real lives and histories and family connections. But really, they’re people just like us, and some of them have identical twins! It’s a fairly unusual thing for anyone to have a genetically identical body double, but it’s all the weirder when one twin is a famous actor in TV or movies. That’s exactly the case for this list. With a couple of exceptions, most of these twins have one famous sibling, and another who isn’t very well known (except as the twin of the star!). Some of these might surprise you. Here are actors you didn’t know had an identical twin.
