A British man has decided to pack up his life in the UK and take a job at Paulatuk, a remote Inuit village deep in Northern Canada. He has to get there though and the only way is a one-way flight across the freezing tundras. This flight is fraught with danger and a increasingly irate passenger means that the pilots have to be their most skilled to pull this one off.
