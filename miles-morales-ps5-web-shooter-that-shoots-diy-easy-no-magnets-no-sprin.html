How to print the templates:
1. Download Adobe Reader here: https://acrobat.adobe.com/us/en/acrobat/pdf-reader.html
2. Right click on the template, then "Open With" Adobe Reader
3. Make sure the scale is set to 100% (FYI these templates were designed to fit my wrist which has a circumference of roughly 5.5 inches, so you may need to elongate the wrist strap piece if your wrist is larger than mine)
4. Click "Page Setup…" and specify what type of paper you are using (US Letter for example) before printing

P.S. If the template pieces get cut off the page, then print in the “Poster” format and set the "Overlap" to 0 in. Doing this will accurately split all the contents up among multiple pages
