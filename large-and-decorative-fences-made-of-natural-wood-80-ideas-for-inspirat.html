Large and decorative fences made of natural wood! 80 ideas for inspiration!

Natural wood fences are very popular with both the owners of suburban areas and urban residents. Despite this, there are those who consider wood to be a cheap and boring design option. But this is far from the case. Wood is the most valuable, natural material that will emphasize the refined taste and ideally complement the plot of land.

Types of wooden fences:
- A classic fence is a fence made of wooden planks installed with a solid canvas. The canvas can be both horizontal and vertical.
- A palisade is a strictly vertical canvas made of boards pointed at the top. This option performs the best protective function, since it is not so easy to climb over sharp stakes.
- Log paving is logs stacked close to each other. Logs can be placed both horizontally and vertically. For better adhesion between them, the logs are sawn lengthwise.
- A picket fence is a traditional fence for a garden or a summer cottage. This option is one of the cheapest wooden fences.
- Herringbone or ladder is a way of laying boards horizontally and overlapping. Such a fence is absolutely not transparent and, at the same time, is well ventilated.
- A wattle fence is a fence made of branches of bushes or trees.
- A lattice is a decorative type of a fence. Boards can be positioned both straight and at an angle. Such a hedge is most often used to decorate the site.
