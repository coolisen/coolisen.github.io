About "Valley of The king":
"A quest of legends is ahead, unknown to them what lies waiting beyond the desolate pathway to the entrance of the forest.
On entering this dark, mysterious place, the trees change form while whispering and tormenting. The witches stand guard and the vultures wait for one or more comrade to fall.
