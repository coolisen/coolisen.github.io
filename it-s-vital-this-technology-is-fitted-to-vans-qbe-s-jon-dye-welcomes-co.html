In this video, Jon Dye, Director of Motor at QBE explains the benefits the Euro NCAP and Thatcham Research Commercial Van Ratings will have on the motor insurance industry.

He also reveals how implementing more collision avoidance technology in vans could help keep drivers as well as other road users safe.

Euro NCAP and Thatcham Research have launched the world’s first safety rating for commercial vans.

The results uncover alarming news, with five vans getting a ‘Not Recommended’ rating.
