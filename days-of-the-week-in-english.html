A basic vocabulary lesson for lower level ESL students about the days of the week in English.
(Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday)

- Each day appears on the screen by itself and is said twice.
- Then a list of the seven days appears with their pronunciation given.
- Next there is a simple game of "Which day of the week is missing?" (e.g. Monday, ___, Wednesday). There is a pause before the answer appears.
