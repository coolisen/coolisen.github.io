What do women want? Northside Rocky attempts to answer this question in his music video “Less Important”. Pulling from personal experiences Rocky portrays how he tries to balance his relationship and ambitions of becoming a superstar. Rocky’s love interest played by Mahogany Jones can be seen on MTV’s Wild N’ Out and a variety of music videos. Her experience combined with Rocky’s natural charisma make for the perfect match to show the ups and downs in a relationship in “Less Important”.
