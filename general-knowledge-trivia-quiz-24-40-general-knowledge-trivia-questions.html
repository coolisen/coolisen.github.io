** General Knowledge Pub Quiz Trivia Questions And Answers:

1. What is the capital city of New Zealand?
Wellington

2. True or false? High heeled shoes were originally created for men.
True. Created in 10th century for Persian cavalry stirrup support.

3. What is the second tallest mountain on Earth? It is located along the China-Pakistan border.
K2 or Karakoram 2 (8611 metres or 28251 feet)

4. Who said this famous quote? “If you tell the truth you don’t have to remember anything.” ?
Mark Twain

5. What do you call a group of rabbits?
Fluffle, colony, herd

6. What does the word “fortuitous” mean?
Happening by accident or chance rather than design

7. Guess the country by flag below.
Bangladesh

8. What is the “octothorpe” commonly known as?
The hash or pound sign #

9. True or false? The U.S. Supreme court has its own private basketball court nicknamed “The Highest Court in the Land”.
True!

10. Name all five of the “Great Lakes” of North America.
Superior, Michigan, Huron, Erie, Ontario

11. Guess the scrambled word below.
ACADEMIC

12. Who’s that Pokemon?
Mew

13. What was the shortest war in history and how long did it last?
Anglo-Zanzibar War

14. Riddle: I am easy to lift but hard to throw. What am I?
Feather, Paper, Leaf, etc.

15. What exactly is Jupiter’s “Great Red Spot”?
A high-pressure storm

16. Solve the following multiplication problem
70275

17. Guess the animal by close up.
Ostrich

18. Who said this famous quote? “When words fail, music speaks.” ?
William Shakespeare

19. What kind of foods contain omega-3?
Fish, Nuts & Seeds, Plant oils

20. Which planet in our solar system has the most volcanos with over 1000?
Venus

21. What is Cookie Monster’s real name?
Sid

22. True or false? Earth is a perfect sphere.
False! It’s squished at he poles and bulges at the equator.

23. The pluot is a hybrid of which 2 fruits?
Plum and apricot

24. Guess the scrambled word below.
SENTENCE

25. Which planet’s moon has a has a fully developed atmosphere (~50% denser than Earth’s)?
Saturn’s moon Titan

26. What does the word “amalgamate” mean?
Combine or unite to form one organization or structure.

27. Name all the presidents on Mount Rushmore.
Theodore Roosevelt, George Washington, Abraham Lincoln, Thomas Jefferson

28. What was bubble wrap originally used for?
3D wallpaper

29. What do you call a group of stingrays?
Fever

30. There is a piece of land between Egypt and Sudan named “Bir Tawil”. What is special about it?
Unclaimed by any country.

31. Riddle: What English word has 3 consecutive double letters?
BOOKKEEPER

32. The suffix -stan is a Persian and Urdu word that means “Place” or “Where one stands”. Name as many countries as you can with this suffix.
Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan, Turkmenistan, Afghanistan, and Pakistan.

33. Who said this famous quote? “Normality is a paved road: it’s comfortable to walk but no flowers grow.” ?
Vincent van Gogh

34. Guess the scrambled word below.
DERANGED, GARDENED

35. Solve the following addition problem
11455

36. What is “aulophobia” the fear of?
Flutes

37. What is the tallest mountain on Mars called? And approximately how many times taller is it than Mount Everest on Earth?
Olympus Mons (2.5-3x taller than Mt. Everest)

38. China owns nearly all the pandas in the world. How much do they rent them out for in USD to other countries per year?
~$1M USD / year

39. What does the first P in the programming language “PHP” stand for?
The P stands for PHP

40. What is the scientific term “borborygmus” more commonly known as?
Stomach growling or rumbling

Thanks for playing the General Knowledge Trivia Quiz! How much did you know?
