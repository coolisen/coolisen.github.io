About me:
※※※※※※※※
I am a young artist from France and I have been writing songs since I was 15 years old. Music is a real outlet for me, as it allows me to capture feelings, thoughts, and experiences that could not be described with words. As of today, I have released two self-made EPs and a 10-track album called “NOGAS”. Since then, I have played several shows opening for different acts such as Bagarre and Tambour Battant. My last singles "They May", "I Am You" and "Halfway Around The World" will take you on a journey through my universe, depicted by a blend of Rock, Folk, Electro and Hip Hop.
