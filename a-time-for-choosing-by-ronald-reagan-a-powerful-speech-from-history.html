Ronald Reagans greatest speech ‘A Time for Choosing’ was first delivered on national television on October 27, 1964. What few viewers knew that day was that Reagan was not relying on a script or Teleprompter.

The speech showed that Reagan was an independent thinker who knew what he wanted to say, and it paved the way for one of the most consequential political careers in American history.

For years afterwards, Americans would recall the first time they saw their future president, not as a Hollywood actor but as leader of a conservative movement and future chief executive of the country.
