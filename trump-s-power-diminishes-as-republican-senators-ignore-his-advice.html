Donald Trump urged Republicans in the Senate last week to hold the line and not allow the debt ceiling to be raised. They ignored his calls, and 11 Republicans joined the Democratic majority in a cloture vote that then allowed passage of legislation to extend the ceiling through December. This isn’t the first time that Senate Republicans have ignored Donald’s advice, and it could be a sign that his influence and power are waning. Ring of Fire’s Farron Cousins discusses this.
