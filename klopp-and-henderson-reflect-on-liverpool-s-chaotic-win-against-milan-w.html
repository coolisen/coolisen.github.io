Liverpool’s Champions League opener was a rollercoaster 3-2 victory against Milan at Anfield. Despite taking an early lead and dominating the first half, the hosts trailed 2-1 at half-time before goals by Mo Salah and Jordan Henderson grabbed victory.  ‘It was a brilliant game … with 10 or 15 minutes where we lost a little bit the plot,’ said the Liverpool manager Jürgen Klopp.
