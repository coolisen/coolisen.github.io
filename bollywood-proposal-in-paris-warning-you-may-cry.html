The Backstory: My parents told me our flight from Delhi got rerouted from Frankfurt to Paris, my clueless self didn’t think much of it and decided to use our “layover” to shop. As soon as we walked out of the Paris airport, a man (who turned out to be our amazing videographer) was holding a sign with my name and handed me the first note from Shyam… watch the rest to see the magic unfold!!

To my best friend, lover boy and now fiancé, I love you!!! You bring out the best and weirdest in me, support my career like it’s your own and truly make me feel like the happiest girl in the world.
