►IELTS Speaking Part 2 Questions and Answers: Topic 16 - Sporting event
Describe a sporting event that you enjoyed watching.
You should say:
- What the event was
- Where you watched it
- Who watched this event with you
And explain why you enjoyed watching this event

2. What kinds of sports facilities exits in your community?
