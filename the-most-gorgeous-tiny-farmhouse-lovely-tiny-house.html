The Most Gorgeous Tiny Farmhouse

Price: $88/ night
Location: Mt. Juliet, Tennessee, United States
Size: 4 guests · 1 bedroom · 2 beds · 1 bath

This Tiny Home is in the style of a farmhouse, on 12.3 secluded acres and the lake directly across the street! This place is great for hunting and fishing. Only 30 minutes from downtown Nashville, and 15-20 minutes from all major cities that have anything you will need! Absolutely NO smoking! The Key code will always be the last 4 digits of your phone number!
Guest access
Lamar hill access area
Other things to note
Door Keypad entry will be the last 4 digits of your phone number that you book with! hold down on screen until numbers light up, sometimes there will be 2 numbers that pop up first. Just hit these numbers and then it will show the entire number pad. From there, just type in your code! to lock back just hit the lock icon.

There are cameras on the property, this is just to see who pulls up and who enters and leaves the tiny home. There are no cameras inside or any that can see inside. There are 4 cameras, one on the side where the flood light is, one on the blue house facing the tiny home, one on top of the tiny home facing the back field and one on the front porch of the blue house facing the road.

there will be a $500 fee if evidence of smoking is found inside the tiny home, if you must smoke, please step away from the tiny home and dispose of butts in fire pit
