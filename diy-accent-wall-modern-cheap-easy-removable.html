In this video I install a custom DIY Accent Wall.

Overall this was one of the easier projects I have completed recently. After my wife found this idea on Pinterest we came up with our plan. For our accent wall design we used MDF trim in a chevron pattern. All angled boards were installed at a 30 degree angle. Only a few tools are needed for this project including a miter saw, air nailer, paint bush & rollers, tape measure, level, nail punch, and some other small things.

The major part of this project that I am proud of is the fact that it will be removable with very minor fixes to the wall. This is awesome just in case this is no longer a cool thing to do in the future.
