In this highly speculative video, I discuss the legal ramifications of Tesla cars, and eventual Tesla robots (Teslabot) becoming smarter and more self-sufficient (or quasi-sentient). Will we have to take our Tesla cars or Tesla bots to court? If they’re found guilty of a crime, how can we punish them? Let’s take a walk down speculation lane and consider these matters!
