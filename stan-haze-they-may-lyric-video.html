About me:
※※※※※※※※
I am a young artist from France and I have been writing songs since I was 15 years old. Music is a real outlet for me, as it allows me to capture feelings, thoughts, and experiences that could not be described with words. As of today, I have released two self-made EPs and a 10-track album called “NOGAS”. Since then, I have played several shows opening for different acts such as Bagarre and Tambour Battant.
