Moe also decided to take some time to talk about other relevant League topics.

Akshan has been out for about a month now in League of Legends. He started off with a pretty bad win rate and public perception with most people thinking he was a garbage champion. But now that players, and Moe, have had more time to figure Akshan out, Moe can give a more honest and accurate opinion on the champion.

On Sunday, the 2021 LCS season came to a conclusion with a new team hanging up their banner in the LCS studio… 100 THIEVES! With this being the end of the season, Moe decided to give his thoughts on the overall 2021 LCS season and breakdown the playoffs to see how 100 Thieves took the Summoner’s Cup!
Finally, with Moe running away to Mexico and trying to grind on the LAN server from Unranked to Challenger.
