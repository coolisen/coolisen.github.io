The tool provides users with movement feedback and can be used with specific drills and exercises, as well as when hitting shots in order to train and strengthen your core muscles. It will hold your spine in position for better rotation, more power, and improved consistency.

Used daily by everyone from beginners to pros, the TPro could really revolutionise your game, as you train for high quality golf posture.
