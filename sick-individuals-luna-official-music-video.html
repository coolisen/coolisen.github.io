Listen here ▶️ http://revr.ec/LUNA

SICK INDIVIDUALS are back for more action on Revealed Recordings, with New track ‘Luna’ to be released on the label as a follow up to previous outings such as ‘Kodi’, which came out in early 2018 and has garnered over 5 million plays on Spotify to date, a feat that ‘Luna’ could be set to surpass! They have also played numerous sets and major festivals such as Tomorrowland, Ultra Music Festival and Creamfields to name a few!

The track begins with an ethereal tone before the blend of big beats and progressive house melodies kicks in when the bass drops. ‘Luna’ has a cinematic vibe throughout the whole track and the commanding vocal used will only help SICK INDIVIDUALS dominate any live arena they perform!

The last release on the label from stalwarts SICK INDIVIDUALS came in the form of ‘Symphony (feat Nevve)’, which was supported by the likes of label boss Hardwell, Blasterjaxx and Tom & Jame and also more recently remixed by Dr Phunk, and ‘Luna’ is guaranteed to ensure the ever-growing list of supporters continues to increase with every output. Grab your copy of ‘Luna’ from SICK INDIVIDUALS when it is released 29th March via Revealed Recordings.
