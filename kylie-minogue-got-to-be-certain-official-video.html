“Got To Be Certain” became the second single in Australian history to enter at number one and was a top 10 hit in Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, New Zealand and the United Kingdom where it made number 2 in May 1988. The video was filmed in her native Melbourne and was directed by Chris Langman. Produced and written by Stock Aitken Waterman, the track features on her 7 x UK platinum debut album “Kylie”.
