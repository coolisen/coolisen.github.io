The poet Rumi once wrote, “Out beyond ideas of rightdoing and wrongdoing, there is a field. I’ll meet you there.” The realm of right and wrong, Sadhguru explains, is necessary for conducting the affairs of the world, but if you are stuck in this realm and want to rule the inner kingdom, you will never get there.
