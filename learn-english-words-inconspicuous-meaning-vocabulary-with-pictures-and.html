What does Inconspicuous mean? Learn the meaning of Inconspicuous as we define this advanced vocabulary word with a simple definition, pictures, example sentences, English pronunciation and audio.

Inconspicuous definition (adjective) not easily observed

Inconspicuous pronunciation: in-kuh n-spik-yoo-uh s

Examples of Inconspicuous in a sentence:

1. The celebrity hoped she looked inconspicuous so her fans would not approach her in the grocery store.

2. When the shoplifter walked out of the store, he did his best to appear inconspicuous.

3. As soon as the kitten saw the big dog, it ran under a bed and tried to look inconspicuous.

4. Sheila and Keith tried to appear inconspicuous as they snuck out of the school building.

5. Janice was so quiet and inconspicuous in the corner I did not realize she was in the room.
