Saweetie chats with ET’s Rachel Smith at the 2021 Met Gala. The rapper admits she’s ‘nervous’ about making her debut appearance at the iconic fashion event. Saweetie opens up about the special meaning behind her dress’ cape. A nod to her heritage, Saweetie says, ‘My cape represents the Black American heritage flag and the Filipino flag.’
