Aaron Cobo is now a part of BLUNT Flow team !
Aaron is a young Spanish shredder that lives in Catalonia. He’s been riding on BLUNT in the Spanish streets for many years, and today we made his dream come true by introducing him into our BLUNT Europe Flow Team.
For his welcome video, he wanted to make a full street part and show us his guts ! He teamed up with Matteo Mencuccini, our Pro rider Ivan Jimenez, Martin Duchamp, and some local filmers (Pablo Rivera, Quim Castells, Justin Diaz, Jesus Lopez) in order to make the best street part around Spain. He showed us what he’s capable of and we look forward to work on new projects with him !
