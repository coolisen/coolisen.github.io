We dance Contact improvisation in Bulgaria. Here we are in Varna, sunny city by the sea. We dance in the dancehall and under the open sky, in the grass and in the sand. We make our videos to share our smiles and our discoveries with all of you.



In this video we are dancing Contact Improvisation in the Sea Garden in Varna, Bulgaria, 29 April 2010



What is it - Contact Improvisation?

Dance, art, sports, meditation, therapy, self-improvement method, a way of communicating, acrobatics, martial arts? All this together … and something more?



Contact improvisation (CI) is a dance technique in which points of physical contact provide the starting point for exploration through movement improvisation. Contact Improvisation is a form of dance improvisation and is one of the best-known and most characteristic forms of postmodern dance.
