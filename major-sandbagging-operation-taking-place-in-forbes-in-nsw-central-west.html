Residents of roughly 800 homes in Forbes, in the Central West of New South Wales, have been warned to prepare for a possible evacuation.

Preparations are taking place in the town, with a major sandbagging operation being seen.

It comes as a major flood peak is due to arrive in the town on the Lachlan River.
